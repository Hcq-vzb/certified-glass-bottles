About KIWL

Company Profile

Our company is a specialist in selling and exporting packaging materials to the domestic and international alcoholic beverage industry. Our main products include all kinds of glass bottles, caps, stoppers, labels, paper tube box. Our products are exported to more than 70 countries and regions in the world. We are a joint group of factories and manufacturers in the packaging industry, including glass bottle factories, cap factories, screw cap factories, label factories and manufacturers of paper tube box factories, as well as other co-operative factories of promotional products for alcoholic beverages. The aim of the group is to establish a real platform of one-stop packaging solution provider. The advantage of our group work is to combine different leading factories in the packaging field into one solid group, we all focus on the team work from different fields with the same goal of providing professional packaging solutions to satisfy our customers' needs in order to obtain a stable long-term business partnership.

For a Sustainable Future Together With KIWL

Bottle cap environmental protection actions can also drive extensive participation from all walks of life. Schools can hold bottle cap environmental protection creative competitions to inspire children's creativity and environmental protection enthusiasm; companies can promote the importance of bottle cap recycling on product packaging and establish a recycling reward mechanism to encourage consumers to actively participate; public welfare organizations can organize large-scale bottle cap recycling activities to increase society's attention and action on bottle cap environmental protection.
Small bottle caps can play a huge role in the road to environmental protection as long as we treat them with care. Let us start with each bottle cap, gather our strength, and jointly paint a picture of a green and sustainable future.
