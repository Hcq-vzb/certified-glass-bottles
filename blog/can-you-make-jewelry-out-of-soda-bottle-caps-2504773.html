Can you make jewelry out of soda bottle caps?

Jan 14, 2026

Leave a message

Sophia Brown
Sophia is a marketing manager at Jiangsu KIWL Machinery Manufacturing Group Co., Ltd. She is responsible for promoting the company's aluminum caps, screw caps, GPI caps, and non - refillable caps to over 100 international alcoholic beverage brands. Her creative marketing strategies have significantly increased the company's market share.

Hey there! As a supplier of soda bottle caps, I often get asked if it's possible to make jewelry out of these little guys. Well, let me tell you, the answer is a resounding yes! In this blog, I'm gonna share with you all the ins and outs of turning soda bottle caps into beautiful pieces of jewelry.

Why Soda Bottle Caps?

First off, you might be wondering why anyone would want to use soda bottle caps for jewelry. Well, there are a bunch of reasons. For starters, they're super accessible. You can find them just about anywhere – at home, at the park, in the recycling bin. They're also incredibly versatile. With a little creativity, you can transform them into earrings, necklaces, bracelets, and even rings.

Another great thing about soda bottle caps is that they're eco - friendly. By using them to make jewelry, you're giving them a second life instead of letting them end up in a landfill. It's a small way to do your part for the environment.

Materials You'll Need

Before you start crafting, you'll need to gather a few materials. Here's a basic list:

  • Soda bottle caps: Of course, you can get these from your own soda cans or buy them in bulk from a supplier like me. Check out our 33x47mm Aluminum Plastic Bottle Cap, Wine Cap, and 28mm Orange Aluminum Bottle Caps for some great options.
  • Glue: A strong adhesive like E6000 or a hot glue gun works well.
  • Jump rings and earring hooks: These are used to attach the bottle caps to chains or other jewelry findings.
  • Beads and charms: To add some extra bling to your jewelry.
  • Sandpaper: To smooth the edges of the bottle caps.
  • Paint and brushes: If you want to customize the color of your bottle caps.

Preparing the Bottle Caps

Once you've got your materials, it's time to prep the bottle caps. Start by cleaning them thoroughly with soap and water to remove any dirt or residue. Then, use sandpaper to smooth the edges. This will make the bottle caps more comfortable to wear and prevent any sharp edges from scratching your skin.

If you want to change the color of the bottle caps, you can paint them. Just make sure to use paint that's suitable for metal. Apply a few coats of paint, letting each coat dry completely before applying the next one. You can also add patterns or designs using stencils or free - hand painting.

Making Different Types of Jewelry

Earrings

Making earrings out of soda bottle caps is a breeze. First, use a drill or a nail to make a small hole near the top of each bottle cap. Then, attach an earring hook to the hole using a jump ring. You can leave the bottle caps as they are or add some beads and charms for extra flair.

Necklaces

For necklaces, you can create a pendant using a single bottle cap or string multiple bottle caps together. To make a pendant, attach a jump ring to the top of the bottle cap and then connect it to a chain. If you're stringing multiple bottle caps, you can use wire or cord to thread them together. Add some beads and charms between the bottle caps to make the necklace more interesting.

Bracelets

Bracelets can be made in a similar way to necklaces. You can string bottle caps on a stretchy cord or use a chain and connect the bottle caps with jump rings. You can also make a cuff bracelet by shaping a single bottle cap or a few connected bottle caps into a circular shape.

Tips and Tricks

  • Get creative with the designs: You can cut the bottle caps into different shapes, layer them, or combine them with other materials like fabric or wood.
  • Use a sealant: If you've painted the bottle caps, apply a clear sealant to protect the paint and make it last longer.
  • Experiment with different sizes and colors: Mixing and matching different types of bottle caps can create a unique and eye - catching look.
