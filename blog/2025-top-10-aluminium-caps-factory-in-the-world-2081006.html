Introduction to Aluminium Caps

Aluminium caps are closures widely used in various industries, especially in the beverage and pharmaceutical sectors. They are known for their excellent properties such as corrosion - resistance, light - weight, and high formability. Aluminium caps can be easily customized in terms of shape, size, color, and printing, which makes them suitable for different packaging requirements. They provide a reliable seal to protect the contents from contamination, oxidation, and leakage, ensuring the quality and safety of the products inside the containers.
