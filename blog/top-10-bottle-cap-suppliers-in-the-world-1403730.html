Top 10 Bottle Cap Suppliers in the world

Oct 14, 2025

Leave a message

Michael Davis
Michael is a production supervisor at Jiangsu KIWL Machinery Manufacturing Group Co., Ltd. With 20 + years of experience in the manufacturing field, he manages the production line efficiently to ensure timely delivery of high - quality products to customers around the world.

Hey there! As a bottle cap supplier myself, I've been in the thick of this industry for quite some time. It's been an exciting journey, filled with ups and downs, but one thing's for sure - the world of bottle caps is vast and full of interesting players. Today, I'm gonna share with you the top 10 bottle cap suppliers in the world.

1. Crown Holdings

Crown Holdings is a giant in the packaging industry, and their bottle cap division is no exception. They've got a wide range of products, from simple metal caps to high - tech, tamper - evident closures. What I really like about them is their commitment to innovation. They're always coming up with new designs that not only look great but also serve a practical purpose. For example, their caps are designed to keep the contents fresh for longer, which is a huge plus for beverage and food companies.

2. Amcor

Amcor is another well - known name. They're all about sustainability, which is a hot topic these days. Their bottle caps are made from recyclable materials, and they're constantly working on reducing their environmental impact. They also offer a diverse range of cap styles, suitable for different types of bottles, whether it's a glass wine bottle or a plastic water bottle.

3. Silgan Holdings

Silgan Holdings has a reputation for producing high - quality bottle caps at a reasonable price. They've got a large manufacturing capacity, which means they can handle big orders without any issues. Their caps are known for their reliability and durability. Whether it's a carbonated beverage or a pharmaceutical product, Silgan's caps can do the job.

4. Guala Closures

Guala Closures is a specialist in the wine and spirits industry. Their caps are often seen on high - end bottles, adding a touch of elegance. They offer a variety of closures, including corks and screw caps. Their Top Embossed Wooden Spirit Bottle Cork is a real standout, with beautiful embossing that makes the bottle look even more luxurious.

5. Bericap

Bericap is a global leader in plastic closures. They focus on producing caps that are easy to use and provide a good seal. Their caps are used in a wide range of industries, from food and beverage to household chemicals. They're also big on research and development, constantly looking for ways to improve their products.

6. Closure Systems International (CSI)

CSI is all about customization. They work closely with their customers to create unique bottle cap solutions. Whether it's a special shape, color, or branding, CSI can make it happen. Their Top Embossed Zinc Alloy Spirit Bottle Cork is a great example of their ability to create high - end, customized closures.

7. Alcoa Closure Systems International

Alcoa is known for its expertise in aluminum closures. Their caps are lightweight yet strong, and they're often used in the beverage industry. They've got a long history of innovation, and their products are trusted by many big brands.

8. Anchor Glass Closures Systems

Anchor Glass specializes in glass closures. Their caps are not only functional but also add a touch of class to the bottle. They're often used for premium products, like high - end perfumes or specialty wines.

9. O.Berk Company

O.Berk Company offers a wide range of packaging solutions, including bottle caps. They've got a great selection of standard and custom caps. Their 33*47mm Non - refillable Aluminum Plastic Cap is a popular choice for products that require a non - refillable feature, like some pharmaceutical or alcoholic beverages.

10. Rexam Closures

Rexam Closures is a well - established player in the market. They've got a strong presence in Europe and the Americas. Their caps are known for their quality and performance. They offer a variety of closure types, suitable for different applications.
