Introduction to Bottle Caps

Bottle caps are essential components in the packaging industry. They serve multiple crucial functions, including sealing the contents of a bottle to prevent leakage, contamination, and spoilage. They also play a significant role in brand identity, as many companies use unique bottle cap designs to enhance their product's visual appeal and distinguish themselves from competitors. Bottle caps come in various materials such as plastic, metal, and cork, and different types like screw - on caps, snap - on caps, and child - resistant caps, catering to a wide range of industries including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.
