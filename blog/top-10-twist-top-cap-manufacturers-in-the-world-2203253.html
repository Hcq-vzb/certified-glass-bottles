Introduction to Twist Top Caps

Twist top caps, also known as screw caps, are a popular type of closure for various containers. They offer a convenient and reliable way to seal products, preventing leakage and maintaining the freshness of the contents. These caps are widely used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and household chemicals. The design of twist top caps allows for easy opening and closing, making them user - friendly. They come in different materials, including plastic, metal, and a combination of both, and can be customized in terms of size, color, and printing to meet the specific needs of different products.
