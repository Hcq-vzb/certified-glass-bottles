Quality Management

Quality is in Everything We do

We take satisfaction in being an ISO9001,ISO14001,ISO22000-certified manufacturer with FSC/Sedex/BSCl/Intertek-certified bottles cap label and paper box. Our manufacturing and quality control teams collaborate seamlessly throughout the cap manufacturing process to ensure the finished product meets our high standards. Furthermore, we are equipped to comply with many nations' export requirements and certificates.

 
 
 
 

 

Witness Quality Inside Out

 

 
Safety and wear resistance
When our bottle caps are used to package food products, their materials must be non-toxic and harmless to ensure the integrity of the bottle cap surface
 
Adaptability
The size and shape of the bottle cap are precisely adapted to the bottle mouth and can be tightly combined with the bottle mouth
 
Consistent Quality
With advanced machines and rigorous inspection, we can guarantee consistent quality all the time.

 

 

Work with the Best, For the Best cap & label & paper box

Our supplier audit system is stringent, guaranteeing that we only engage with the industry's best suppliers." To ensure consistent quality and on-time delivery, we carefully choose and source raw materials, secondary processing, and accessories. You may be confident that we prioritize the highest standards for our products.

 

Check, Check, Check for the Best

Rest assured of our commitment to quality. Our production undergoes rigorous inspections before, during, and after completion. Our state-of-the-art infrared detection equipment and experienced workers ensure each process is scrutinized to deliver only the highest quality products.
