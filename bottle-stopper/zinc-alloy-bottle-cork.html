Zinc Alloy Bottle Stopper: Sleek Design, Fully Customizable

Products Feature

Luxurious Texture: The zinc alloy body is finished with a high-gloss metallic plating and precision-polished for a smooth touch and premium visual appeal, instantly elevating the brand image of your bottled products.

Durable & Corrosion-Resistant: The zinc alloy material undergoes an anti-oxidation plating process, delivering exceptional wear and corrosion resistance. It maintains a bright, like-new appearance even with long-term use, making it ideal for long-term storage of spirits and premium beverages.

Reliable Sealing: The natural cork core offers excellent elasticity and sealing performance, creating a tight fit with the bottle mouth to effectively block air, prevent oxidation, and preserve the original flavor of your beverages.

Aesthetic & Functional Design: The streamlined teardrop shape is minimalist yet highly recognizable. The surface can be engraved with your brand logo, fulfilling both practical sealing needs and acting as a visual brand symbol to enhance brand recall.
