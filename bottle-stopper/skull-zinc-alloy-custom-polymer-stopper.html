Our Features

 

Unique Design

The fusion of a skull and a snail shell creates an edgy and artistic look. It stands out from ordinary wine stoppers, making it a perfect choice for those who love niche and distinctive items. Whether you're collecting unique barware or looking for a special gift, this stopper will catch people's eyes. It can also be a great conversation starter at parties.

The upper part is made of a durable material that maintains the intricate details of the skull and snail shell design. The bottom silicone part is flexible and provides a good seal.
