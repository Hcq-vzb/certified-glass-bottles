Product Overview

Welcome to our premium custom glass liquor bottle stopper product page! We specialize in providing unique, high-quality wine stopper solutions for global liquor producers. Whether you're looking for modern and stylish glass material, traditional and classic natural cork, or practical and durable agglomerated cork (granulated, micro-granulated) and polymer materials, we can meet your customization needs.

 

Customization Services
 

 

Shape Customization

Based on your brand characteristics and packaging design, we can customize various unique shapes of stoppers to make your products stand out.

Stopper Material Options

  • Glass: Crystal clear, modern and stylish, reusable, environmentally friendly, and practical.
  • Natural Cork: Natural charm, good sealing properties, and breathability.
  • Agglomerated Cork (Granulated, Micro-Granulated): Eco-friendly material, stable performance, suitable for long-term storage.
  • Polymer Materials: Lightweight and durable, waterproof and moisture-resistant, easy to clean and maintain.
