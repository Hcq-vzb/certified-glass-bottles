Product Material

 

 

We offer three premium material choices for the plug body: polymer (synthetic), microgranule, and natural cork, each catering to different functional and aesthetic needs.

Polymer (synthetic) is the most widely used option due to its exceptional stability and durability. Unlike traditional materials, it resists deformation and degradation over time, ensuring long-term performance. All our polymer plugs meet strict food-grade certifications, making them safe for contact with consumable products. Additionally, polymer allows for extensive customization-clients can select from a variety of colors and shapes to suit their branding or functional requirements.For those seeking a more natural and upscale option, we provide **microgranule cork** and **solid oak cork**, both sourced from premium Portuguese oak forests. These materials are renowned for their consistent quality, superior elasticity, and enhanced sealing properties. Microgranule cork, composed of compressed cork granules, offers excellent resilience, while solid oak cork delivers a classic, eco-friendly appeal with reliable performance. Both options are ideal for wine, spirits, and gourmet packaging, where authenticity and material integrity are valued.By offering polymer, microgranule, and oak cork, we ensure that every client finds the perfect balance of functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability for their specific application.

Packaging Of Bottle Stopper

Our standard packaging utilizes 5-ply corrugated cardboard boxes to ensure maximum protection during transit. Each plug is carefully arranged in sequence within the box, with layered separators between each row to prevent friction and damage. Additionally, every plug is individually wrapped in a dust-proof OPP bag to maintain cleanliness and prevent abrasion against the cardboard.

For export shipments or bulk orders, we offer reinforced palletized packaging to meet international shipping requirements. This method enhances stability, facilitates efficient handling, and minimizes the risk of transit-related damage.

Whether for domestic or overseas delivery, our packaging solutions are designed to safeguard product integrity while optimizing logistics efficiency. Custom packaging options, such as branded boxes or specialized cushioning, are also available upon request.
