Products Description

 

 

 

T-shape Zamak Bottle Stoppers

This wine bottle stopper is made from zinc alloy, a durable and sturdy material. The surface has been finely polished and treated to prevent oxidation, giving it an elegant metallic sheen that enhances the overall packaging. The stopper body features an ultra-fine particle structure, offering excellent sealing performance and flexibility. It can tightly seal bottles of various neck diameters, effectively blocking air and slowing down the oxidation process of the wine, thereby maintaining the stability of the wine's flavour. Suitable for red wine, white wine, spirits, and other types of alcohol, it is both practical and decorative, making it an ideal choice for wine packaging and gift pairings.
