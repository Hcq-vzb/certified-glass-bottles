Products Description

 

 

Custom Wooden Top Stopper

These T - shaped bottle stoppers feature a wooden top paired with a cork base, blending natural charm and functional design. The wooden top, polished to a smooth finish and showcasing visible natural grain, boasts intricate engravings: dark brown etchings spell out some words along a circumferential band, while delicate patterns adorn the top surface, adding artistic flair. The cork base ensures a tight, airtight seal, preserving the beverage's flavor and freshness-ideal for craft brews or fruit - infused drinks, hinted by the "orange peel" motif. Beyond practicality, this design serves as a unique brand showcase, merging nature - inspired aesthetics with meticulous craftsmanship, perfect for elevating premium beverage packaging.

Packing and Transportation

 

 

 

For packaging, we employ five-layer corrugated cartons, each fitted with an OPP bag to keep products clean. To prevent rubbing between cap layers, separator paper is inserted between each tier.

 

When it comes to pallet stacking, we supply plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, tailored to customer requirements. Paper corner protectors are added around the goods, along with a top plate, and the entire stack is shrink-wrapped to enhance protection.
