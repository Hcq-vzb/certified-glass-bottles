1. Material & Craftsmanship

Crafted with premium beech wood tops and durable high - polymer bodies, these T - shape plugs showcase fine craftsmanship. We offer extensive customization: choose from micro/ultra - micro particle bodies, oak options, or multiple sizes. Laser engraving, screen printing, and spraying on the wood top let you add unique branding. Low MOQs and quick prototyping make us an ideal partner for more orders.
