Product Introduction

The champagne wire basket, also known as a metal coil or wire fastener, is designed to firmly hold the cork inside a champagne bottle. Mainly made of materials such as iron wire, it is carefully shaped to closely surround the cork and the bottleneck. This secure encirclement ensures that the cork remains in its place, keeping an airtight seal intact.

 

The key function of this wire basket is to protect the integrity of the bottle's closure. Whether during transportation, storage, or any handling activity, it prevents the cork from loosening or falling off. Champagne contains a high level of carbonation. If the cork loosens, the carbon dioxide will escape, causing the champagne to lose its fizz and unique flavor, and severely reducing its quality. Therefore, the champagne wire basket is of great importance in protecting the taste and overall quality of the champagne.

Precautions for Use:

 

Before using the wire hoop, carefully inspect it to make sure it's in good condition. Check for any signs of deformation, rust, or other defects. If any issues are found, replace it right away.

 

When securing the champagne cork, ensure the wire hoop fits around the cork correctly. It shouldn't be overly tight, as this may deform the cork and affect the sealing performance. Nor should it be too loose, as that could cause the cork to loosen or come off easily.

 

While storing champagne, refrain from subjecting it to excessive shaking or vibration. This helps prevent weakening the fastening of the wire hoop. Also, be cautious to keep the champagne away from direct sunlight and high - temperature environments, as these can adversely affect the champagne's quality.
