Product Introduction

 

The champagne wire hoop, also referred to as a metal coil or wire basket, is designed to stabilize the cork in a champagne bottle. Made predominantly from metals such as iron wire, it fits snugly between the cork and bottleneck, ensuring the cork remains firm and the seal remains tight.

 

The primary function of this hoop is to maintain the security of the bottle's closure, preventing it from loosening or coming apart during handling and storage. Champagne, which contains a significant amount of carbon dioxide, would lose its bubbles and flavor if the cap became loose, compromising its quality. Therefore, the champagne wire hoop is crucial for preserving the beverage's taste and quality.

Precautions for Use:

 

Prior to use, inspect the wire hoop for completeness and ensure it is free from deformation, rust, or any other defects. If any issues are identified, replace it immediately.

 

When securing the champagne cork, make sure the wire hoop fits snugly around it, neither excessively tight nor excessively loose. Excessive tightness may distort the cork, impacting the sealing performance; excessive looseness may readily result in loosening or detachment.

 

When storing champagne, refrain from excessive shaking or vibration to avoid compromising the securing effect of the wire hoop. Furthermore, be cautious of avoiding direct sunlight and high-temperature conditions, as they can adversely affect the champagne's quality.
