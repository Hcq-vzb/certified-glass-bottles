Products Description

 

Aluminum Top Polymer Plug

This Aluminum Top Polymer Plug features a sleek aluminum cap combined with a high-performance polymer stopper body, offering both visual appeal and reliable sealing. The aluminum top can be customized with laser engraving, screen printing, or spray coating, making it ideal for branding and personalization.
The stopper body is available in multiple material options, including micro-particle, ultra-fine particle, and oak-infused polymer, each designed to provide superior sealing and durability. These materials ensure long-lasting performance, preserving the freshness of wine, spirits, or other bottled liquids.
With a focus on both aesthetics and function, this plug is suitable for premium packaging, promotional products, and customized gift sets. It's a versatile and professional solution for clients seeking quality, customization, and consistency in their bottle closures.

 

Products Details

 

Material

Produced in our China factory, this plug combines a polished aluminum cap with a polymer stopper core. Designed for premium wine and spirit bottles, the conical polymer stopper delivers a secure, airtight seal. It effectively blocks oxygen and contaminants, extending shelf life. Ideal for clients needing reliable closure solutions for export-grade bottling.
