Products Description

 

T-shape Wooden Bottle Cork

This T-shaped wooden bottle cork combines a wooden top with a polymer stopper body, offering both durability and customization potential. The wooden top can be enhanced with various decorative techniques, including silk-screen printing, spray coating, laser engraving, and inlaid wood veneers, allowing for unique branding or personalization.
The stopper body is also available in multiple materials: fine particle, ultra-fine particle, and oak, each providing different levels of sealing performance and texture. Fine particle offers a reliable seal for general use, while ultra-fine particle ensures superior airtightness for long-term storage. The oak variant adds a traditional touch, ideal for premium wine bottles.
This combination of materials and processes makes the cork not only functional in preserving wine freshness but also highly versatile in appearance and application. It's suitable for both commercial bottling and personalized gifts, meeting diverse aesthetic and practical needs.
