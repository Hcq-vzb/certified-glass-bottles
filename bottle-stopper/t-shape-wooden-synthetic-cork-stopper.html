Product Details

 

Wood Selection

The wood on the top is sourced from sustainable forest farms, such as cherry wood with beautiful grains or durable maple wood. After natural air - drying and fine processing, it retains its natural texture while enhancing the stability of the wood, making it less likely to deform or crack.

Sealing Material

The lower sealing part is made of medical - grade nitrile rubber, which has excellent chemical resistance and elasticity. It can closely fit various shapes of bottle mouths, effectively isolating air and moisture, and maintaining the mellow flavor of the liquor for a long time.

Manufacturing Process

The joint between the wood and the rubber is specially treated, with a tight connection and a natural transition. The overall surface of the cork is smooth without burrs, and every detail has been strictly polished and quality - inspected to ensure superior quality.

 

 

 
 
Customization Processes

Printing

Screen printing or heat transfer printing with environmentally friendly inks is used. It can present rich colors and details on the surface of the cork. Whether it's a simple brand logo or an exquisite artwork, it can be perfectly displayed, and the printing is firm and not easy to fade.

Hot Stamping

Advanced hot - stamping equipment is used to precisely apply gold foil to the designated position on the cork, creating a luxurious and shiny effect. Different colors of hot - stamping materials can be selected, such as classic gold, noble rose gold, etc., adding unique charm to the cork.

Laser Engraving

Through high - precision laser engraving technology, patterns or words with uniform depth and smooth lines can be engraved on the surface of the cork. Exclusive brand stories, commemorative dates, etc. can be customized, making the cork a carrier of emotions and memories.

Embossing

Customized embossing molds are used to press various three - dimensional patterns on the surface of the cork, such as imitation leather patterns, braided patterns, etc., increasing the texture and touch of the cork and bringing a unique visual and tactile experience.

 

 

Application Scope and Advantages
 

Suitable for various liquors such as whisky, brandy, vodka, etc. Its advantage lies in that it not only showcases the simplicity and warmth of natural wood but also achieves a high degree of customization in combination with modern technology, helping brands stand out among numerous competitors and enhancing the added value and brand image of products.
