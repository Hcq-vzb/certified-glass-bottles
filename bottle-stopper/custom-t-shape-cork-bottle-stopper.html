Product Introduction

 

Our T - shaped stoppers are meticulously crafted for spirits packaging, representing the perfect blend of quality and creativity. The top of the stopper comes in a variety of materials, offering you rich options:

Aluminum Foil Material

The aluminum foil top has an excellent metallic texture. It is not only sturdy and durable but also adds a stylish and high - end touch to the product. Its good corrosion resistance ensures that during long - term storage, it effectively protects the wine in the bottle from external factors.

Polymer Material

The top made of polymer material has excellent flexibility and stability. It can fit tightly to the bottle mouth, providing excellent sealing performance. At the same time, it has good chemical resistance and will not react with the wine, maintaining the flavor and quality of the spirits to the greatest extent.

Wood Material

The top made of high - quality wood, with its natural texture and feel, endows the spirits with a natural and rustic charm, creating a unique visual and tactile experience for the product. It is especially suitable for spirits brands that pursue traditional styles and a natural atmosphere.
