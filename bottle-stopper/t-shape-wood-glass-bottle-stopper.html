Products Description

 

 

 

T-Shaped Wood Glass Bottle Stopper 

Designed specifically for high-end wine storage, this T-shaped bottle stopper features an innovative combination of a natural solid wood stopper top and a food-grade polymer stopper body. The hand-polished wooden stopper top is made from premium wood such as French oak or black walnut, which has undergone special anti-corrosion treatment, ensuring that each piece retains its unique natural wood grain. The polymer stopper body features a micro-ventilation channel design that provides a perfect seal while allowing for moderate micro-oxygen exchange, making it particularly suitable for the ageing requirements of wine.

 

Products Details

 

Product Feature

1. Premium Material

The solid wood top cover is made of oak with moderate density, finely polished to reveal natural grain patterns. The surface is treated with food-grade wood wax oil to preserve the breathability of the wood while enhancing durability. The polymer plug has excellent resilience and chemical stability, and does not deteriorate even after prolonged contact with alcohol. The two materials are tightly joined by a patented snap-fit structure, which is both attractive and sturdy.

2. user experience

The T-shaped top is ergonomically designed for a comfortable grip and even force distribution. The wooden surface is slip-resistant, allowing for easy operation even in wet conditions. The insertion angle of the plug has been precisely calculated to ensure that it fits perfectly every time. The overall weight distribution is balanced, making the opening and closing process smooth and seamless.

3. User Experience

Whether it's a ready-to-drink wine for everyday meals or a collectible wine that needs to be aged, this bottle stopper provides the perfect preservation solution. Its elegant appearance complements a variety of wine bottle styles, from classic Bordeaux bottles to modern art bottles. It is particularly suitable for wineries to customise as a standard feature for high-end products, and can also be used as a collectible accessory for wine lovers.

4. Sustainable Philosophy

All raw materials have passed EU food contact material safety certification, and the production process strictly adheres to the ISO quality management system. Samples of each batch of products are retained for long-term performance tracking. We provide a comprehensive after-sales service system, including usage guidance and quality commitments. We believe that a good bottle stopper is not only a practical tool, but also a guardian and respect for the quality of wine.
