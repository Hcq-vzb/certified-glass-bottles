Products Description

 

 

 

T-shape Wooden Top Polymer Cork

Crafted from premium dark hardwood, the cap boasts a refined grain and substantial weight. Paired with a polymer cork body, it features a uniformly smooth surface and moderate elasticity.

Design Highlights: The T-shaped structure ensures a secure fit over bottle necks, while the cork body adapts to various containers. The wooden cap elevates the visual sophistication.

Functional Advantages: The polymer material provides superior sealing properties, preventing air ingress and volatile loss, making it ideal for storing wine, spirits, and artisanal beverages.

Customisation Support: Laser engraving and silk-screen logo printing available. Multiple sizes offered. Dual-sided venting channels help equalise pressure, effectively preventing cork ejection.

A versatile bottle stopper blending natural aesthetics with modern craftsmanship, delivering premium sealing solutions for B2B clients.

FAQ

 

 

01. Is the wooden portion of the bottle stopper cap prone to cracking or warping?

The wooden cap is crafted from premium beech wood (alternative woods are also available), undergoes kiln-drying and moisture-proof treatment, and possesses excellent resistance to deformation and cracking. During regular use, simply avoid prolonged immersion or exposure to direct sunlight to maintain its aesthetic appeal and structural integrity. Each product undergoes rigorous quality inspection prior to dispatch from our factory to ensure compliance with durability standards.

02.Can mixed batch orders be placed for different bottle stopper sizes?

Yes, we accommodate mixed batch orders across multiple sizes with flexible minimum order quantities, catering to diverse B2B client requirements. You may select specifications according to specific applications, such as wine bottles, spirits bottles, or condiment bottles. For detailed size listings and mixed batch policies, please consult our sales representatives. We shall provide tailored procurement solutions.

03.How effective is the seal of this bottle stopper?

This wooden stopper features a high-performance polymer core, offering exceptional elasticity and sealing properties. It effectively prevents liquid leakage and gas evaporation, making it suitable for the short-term and medium-term storage of various liquids including wine, spirits, and craft beverages.
