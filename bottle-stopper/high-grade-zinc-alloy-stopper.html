Products Description

Zinc Alloy Bottle Stoppers: Crafted to Your Vision 

This zinc alloy bottle stopper features an exquisite gold tree-patterned relief as its visual centerpiece, with a half-moon shape that blends artistry and a sense of ceremony. Paired with a natural cork inner core, it ensures a reliable seal while adding a touch of luxury that elevates your bottle's brand identity.

We offer full customization services:

Appearance Customization: Adjust the relief pattern, shape outline, and surface plating (e.g., gold plating, antique bronze, matte black, etc.) to create an exclusive visual symbol that aligns with your brand.

Detail Customization: Engrave your brand logo, slogan, or unique textures on the base to achieve comprehensive brand integration from sight to touch.

Size Adaptation: Customize the cork inner core for different bottle mouth sizes, ensuring perfect sealing and long-lasting durability.

Whether you're creating an exclusive closure for premium spirits, limited-edition wines, or a branded gift accessory, we can turn your design ideas into a functional and collectible bottle stopper.
