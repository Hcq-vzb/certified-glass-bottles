Laser Engraving

Laser engraving on zinc alloy stoppers delivers precise, custom logos or textures with 0.1mm detail. The permanent, wear-resistant marks boost brand identity without damaging the alloy's structure, ideal for premium, long-lasting appeal.

Electroplating

Electroplating coats zinc alloy stoppers with protective metal layers, enhancing corrosion resistance. Finishes like gold or nickel add luxury, smooth imperfections, and align with high-end branding, extending lifespan and visual allure.

Polishing

Polishing refines zinc alloy stoppers, removing burrs for a smooth surface. It ensures even electroplating and crisp engravings, while a silky feel elevates user experience, balancing aesthetics and functionality.

Packing and Transportation

 

For packaging, we utilize five-ply corrugated cartons, each individually lined with an OPP bag to ensure product cleanliness. Separator paper is placed between every layer of stoppers to avoid interlayer scuffs and scratches.

For pallet stacking, we supply plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, customized to meet specific customer needs. Paper edge protectors are fitted around the cargo, a top board is added on top, and the whole stack is tightly wrapped with stretch film for enhanced protection.

For transportation, we have long-term annual partnerships with international logistics providers, allowing us to deliver high-quality, cost-efficient logistics services.
