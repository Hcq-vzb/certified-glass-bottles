FAQ

 

 

01.Is this stopper compatible with all types of liquor bottles? 

Yes, this T-shape stopper is designed to fit most standard 750ml spirit bottles, including those for whiskey, vodka, gin, and other high-alcohol beverages. However, we recommend checking the bottle neck diameter to ensure a proper fit, especially for custom or non-standard bottles.

02.How effective is the seal? Is it suitable for long-term storage?

This stopper features a high-quality synthetic polymer body that provides an excellent airtight seal, preventing leakage and evaporation. It is suitable for medium to long-term storage, helping to preserve the flavor and quality of the liquor over time.

03.Can the glass top or metal accents be customized?

Yes, both the glass top and metal parts can be customized. Options include logo engraving, color printing, electroplating, and adding decorative metal rings or embossed logos, allowing brands to create a unique and premium look.
