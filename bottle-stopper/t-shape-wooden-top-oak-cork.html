Products Details

 

 
 
Products details

Material

Features a premium beechwood top (distinct grain, warm tactile feel) paired with a polymer plug body (available in micro-grain/ultra-fine grain/oak variants). Manufactured using advanced factory equipment to ensure dimensional accuracy and stability, meeting consistent quality requirements.

Customisation

Supports laser engraving, screen printing, and spray coating techniques for customising logos, text, or patterns. Low minimum order quantities and rapid prototyping accommodate small-batch orders from SME suppliers, enhancing brand differentiation.

Functionality

The polymer stopper body provides superior sealing (airtight and anti-volatility), compatible with diverse containers including wine, spirits, and condiments. Multiple sizes available (e.g., 19.5mm, 21mm) cover various bottle neck specifications, meeting B2B clients' requirements for versatility.
