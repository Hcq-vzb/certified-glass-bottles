Product Features

Environmental Friendliness and Non-toxicity:
Cork stoppers are made of natural materials, non-toxic and odorless, posing no harm to the environment.
They are also recyclable, aligning with modern environmental protection concepts.

 

Customizability and Aesthetics:
T-shape wooden corks can be customized according to customer needs, including size, shape, color, etc.
The exquisite wood grain and color options make them not only practical but also decorative and aesthetically pleasing.

 

Wide Range of Applications:
Apart from sealing wine bottles, T-shape wooden corks can also be used for other liquid containers requiring sealing, such as beverages and condiments.

01

Excellent Sealability

The T-shape design increases the contact area between the cork and the bottle neck, thereby enhancing the sealing effect.
The cork material itself possesses elasticity, enabling it to tightly fit the bottle neck and prevent liquid leakage.

02

Durability and Reusability

Cork stoppers can withstand multiple insertions and removals without easily damaging, making them suitable for long-term use.
The T-shape design increases the structural strength of the stopper, making it more durable

03

Corrosion Resistance and Waterproofness

Cork material naturally possesses corrosion resistance, capable of resisting microbial erosion.
Additionally, it exhibits good waterproofness, effectively preventing external moisture from entering the bottle

04

Elasticity and Cushioning

The elasticity of cork stoppers can cushion changes in pressure inside and outside the bottle, protecting the liquid inside from external influences.
This elasticity also makes the stopper smoother to insert and remove, preventing damage to the bottle neck
