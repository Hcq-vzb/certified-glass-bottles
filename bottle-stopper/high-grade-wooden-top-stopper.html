Packing and Transportation

 

 

 

For packaging, we utilize five-layer corrugated cartons, each lined with an OPP bag to ensure the products stay clean. To avoid friction between layers of caps, separator paper is placed between each layer.

 

When it comes to pallet stacking, we provide plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, customized according to customer needs. Paper corner protectors are installed around the goods, complemented by a top plate, and the entire stack is wrapped with film to offer additional protection.

 

In terms of transportation, we have long-term annual collaborations with international logistics companies, allowing us to deliver high-quality and cost-efficient services. These include express delivery, double customs clearance, sea freight, air freight, as well as various other transportation options.
