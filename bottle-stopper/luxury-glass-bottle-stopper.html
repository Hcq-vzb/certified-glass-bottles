Products Description

 

 

 

Luxury Glass Bottle Stopper

This glass stopper features a high-transparency glass top, meticulously cut and polished to achieve a crystal-clear finish, exuding a modern aesthetic that adds a visual highlight to the wine bottle. The stopper body is made using ultra-fine particle technology, making it soft and elastic, ensuring a tight seal around the bottle opening. This effectively prevents oxidation of the wine and loss of aroma, maintaining the quality and stability of the wine over time. Its design is compatible with various types of red wine, white wine, and spirits bottles, making it an ideal choice for enhancing the quality of the wine and highlighting the brand's style.

 

Products Details

 

I. Material and Craftsmanship

The top of this glass stopper is made of high-quality, highly transparent glass, using precision cutting and polishing techniques to achieve a crystal-like texture, enhancing the product's quality. As a professional factory, we strictly control every step of the production process to ensure that the product combines aesthetics with durability, meeting the stringent requirements of global customers for high-end wine bottle accessories and helping brands establish a premium image.

II. Sealing performance

The stopper is made of ultra-fine particle material, which is soft and highly elastic, allowing it to closely fit various bottle openings. This effectively solves the pain points of traditional stoppers, such as poor sealing, easy air leakage, and wine oxidation. As a reliable supplier, we conduct multiple sealing tests to ensure that the product maintains stable wine quality during long-term storage, extends the life cycle of the wine, and wins the trust of end users.

III. Compatibility
