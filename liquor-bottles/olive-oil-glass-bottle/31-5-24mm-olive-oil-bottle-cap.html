Products Details

 

Product Feature

1. Premium Material

Crafted from food-grade aluminum alloy, this oil bottle cap offers excellent corrosion and oxidation resistance. Its lightweight design reduces overall packaging weight while maintaining exceptional structural strength. The metallic material effectively blocks light and air, preventing oil oxidation and ensuring preserved freshness, flavor, and nutritional value from first use to the last-an ideal choice for premium oils.

2. Freshness Preservation

The innovative dual-layer sealing structure provides outstanding oxygen barrier performance, with an oxygen transmission rate below 0.1cc/day. Precision-engineered threads paired with specialized lining materials create an airtight seal. This effectively delays oil rancidity while preserving the oil's natural taste, aroma, and nutrients-keeping every drop at its best quality.

3. User Experience

The ergonomic opening/closing mechanism ensures smooth, effortless operation with just one hand. The tamper-evident ring guarantees product safety while allowing easy authenticity verification. Optimized thread engagement prevents loosening after repeated use while eliminating leakage-delivering convenience and peace of mind.

4. Aesthetic Design

Featuring a mirror-polished surface for an elegant metallic luster, the cap supports various customization options like hot stamping and laser engraving for brand logos or decorative patterns. Its sleek, modern profile complements any oil bottle design, enhancing product sophistication and shelf appeal.
