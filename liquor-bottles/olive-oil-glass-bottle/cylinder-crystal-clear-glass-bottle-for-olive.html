Cylinder Crystal Clear Glass Bottle for Olive Oil

When refinement meets this precious gift from the Mediterranean - here is our Minimalist Classic Olive Oil Bottle. The bottle features clean, smooth lines, just like a meticulously carved work of art. Without any excessive decorations, it exudes a unique elegance. The classic cylindrical design not only conforms to ergonomics, offering a comfortable grip and easy pouring, but also maximizes the capacity for olive oil in a limited space, reducing storage pressure. Crafted from high-quality transparent glass, it showcases a clear and translucent texture. The well-designed bottle cap ensures the olive oil's seal, preventing air from entering and avoiding oxidation and deterioration, thus keeping every drop of oil fresh with its mellow taste and rich nutritional content for a long time.

Packing and Transportation

 

For packaging, we use five - layer corrugated cartons, each lined with an OPP bag to keep the products clean. To prevent abrasion between layers of caps, separator paper is inserted between each layer.

Regarding pallet stacking, we offer plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, tailored to customer requirements. Paper corner protectors are added around the goods, along with a top plate, and the entire stack is secured with wrapping film for extra protection.

In terms of transportation, we maintain annual partnerships with international logistics agencies, enabling us to provide high - quality and cost - effective services. These include express shipping, double customs clearance, sea freight, air freight, and multiples transportation solutions.
