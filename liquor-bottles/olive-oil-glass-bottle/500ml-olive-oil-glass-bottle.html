Design Drawing

 

1. Core Parameters and Structural Design

This glass bottle complies with the standard Q/371082SHP 001, with a nominal capacity of 520ml (full capacity 520±5ml), a reference weight of 380g, an overall height of 278±15mm, a bottle body diameter of φ60.63±1.2mm, and a flat bottom design with a diameter of 35mm. The bottle neck features a threaded structure (outer diameter φ31.3mm, inner diameter φ28±0.3mm), paired with multiple sealing ring grooves to ensure no leakage risk after sealing; the bottle body has smooth lines, with the shoulder featuring an R21.6/R7 rounded transition, enhancing grip comfort and impact resistance.

2. Quality and Performance Advantages

Precision Control: Mouth plane parallelism ≤ 0.3 mm, vertical axis deviation ≤ 2.5 mm, bottle body ellipticity ≤ 1.4 mm, strictly ensuring assembly compatibility (e.g., matching with pump heads and screw caps);

Strength Assurance: Bottle body thickness ≥1.5mm, bottle bottom thickness ≥3.0mm, excellent thermal shock resistance and internal pressure resistance, capable of withstanding high-temperature filling (e.g., hot filling process) and vibration impacts during long-distance transportation;

Practicality: Flat bottom design facilitates stacking and storage, reducing warehouse space occupancy; standardised bottle mouth dimensions compatible with various universal caps (e.g., plastic screw caps, metal tamper-proof caps).

FAQ

1. Is this 520ml glass bottle suitable for hot filling?

Yes, the bottle is made of high-quality white glass with a body thickness of 1.5mm and a base thickness of 3.0mm. It has excellent heat resistance and can withstand high-temperature hot filling. It is suitable for products that require high-temperature sterilisation, such as olive oil and fruit juice, and will not deform or break after hot filling.
