Products Description

 
Our skull-shaped glass bottles are designed to make your brand stand out. Each bottle features stacked, embossed skull motifs, crafted with precision to create a striking, edgy look that appeals to craft spirits, rum, tequila, and premium liquor brands.

Made from high-quality, food-grade glass, these bottles are available in multiple finishes: clear, frosted/opaque, and custom-colored (orange, black, turquoise, red shown here). The textured surface and intricate details enhance visual appeal while ensuring durability for production and shipping.
