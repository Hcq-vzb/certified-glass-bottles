Products Process

Tube CuttingRaw borosilicate glass tubes are precision-cut to the exact lengths specified for the final products, ensuring dimensional uniformity to enable consistent subsequent processing.

Bottom SealingThe cut tube ends are heated to a molten state and hermetically sealed with professional tools, forming a closed base that shapes the fundamental vessel structure.

Blow MoldingHeated glass is inflated and shaped into custom or irregular forms – whether for artistic, functional or decorative purposes – by combining compressed air with bespoke molds.

AnnealingThe formed glass products are subjected to controlled slow heating and cooling in a dedicated annealing furnace. This crucial step relieves internal stresses, which markedly boosts the glass's mechanical strength, thermal shock resistance and overall durability.
