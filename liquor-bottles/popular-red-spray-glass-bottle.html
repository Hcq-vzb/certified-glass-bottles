Packing and Transportation

Packaging Solutions

To safeguard products during transit, we offer a range of materials:

Cushioning Layers: White tissue, bubble wrap, and foam padding act as buffers for breakable goods, warding off scuffs and impacts that could mar surfaces or cause cracks.

Outer Containers: Corrugated cartons, brown cardboard boxes, and printed color boxes deliver structural stability. Corrugated options excel at bearing bulky or weighty items, while color boxes add a refined, shelf-ready look-ideal for products needing polished retail presentation.

Tailored Inserts: Eggshell trays and custom-molded liners (such as those for glass items) lock goods in position, curbing shifts during transit that might lead to fractures or shattering.

Transportation Options

We partner with reliable logistics networks to offer flexible shipping:

Sea Freight: Budget-friendly for bulky, time-insensitive cargo. Perfect for mass quantities, it uses container vessels to extend global coverage-ideal when urgency takes a backseat to cost.

Railway Transport: Strikes a middle ground between cost and pace, fitting cross-border or intercontinental shipments. A reliable option for heavy loads, it trims delivery time compared to ocean transport while keeping expenses in check.

Air Transportation: Speediest for time-critical shipments. Guarantees rapid transit for pressing requirements, though it comes with a steeper price tag.

From protective packing to choosing the right transport mode, we ensure your products arrive safely and on schedule, no matter the destination.
