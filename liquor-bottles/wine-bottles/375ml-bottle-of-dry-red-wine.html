Bottle body structure: Features a classic 'waisted' silhouette with a smooth transition at the midpoint (R32 radius) and an arched concave base (R50.8), ensuring stable grip while enhancing visual appeal through fluid curves; the bottle opening diameter is approximately 25.4mm, compatible with GPI30 - 1680 standard caps, ensuring reliable sealing performance.

Dimension accuracy: Total height 246.1 ± 1.16 mm, neck height 59.5 mm. Critical dimensions such as the bottle opening and body diameter are specified with tolerances (e.g., φ61.67 ± 1.2) to ensure consistency in mass production. Volume is precisely calibrated (375 ± 4 ml at 20°C) to meet legal metrology requirements.

Detailed considerations: The inner diameter of the bottle opening is a minimum of 17.27 mm, combined with a multi-threaded structure to enhance sealing performance; the bottle body has uniform thickness, balancing strength and lightweight design, suitable for industrial filling, transportation, and storage.
