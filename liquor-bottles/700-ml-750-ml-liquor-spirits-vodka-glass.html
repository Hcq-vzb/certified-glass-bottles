Packing&Delivery

For packaging, we use five - layer corrugated cartons, each lined with an OPP bag to keep the products clean. To prevent abrasion between layers of caps, separator paper is inserted between each layer.

Regarding pallet stacking, we offer plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, tailored to customer requirements. Paper corner protectors are added around the goods, along with a top plate, and the entire stack is secured with wrapping film for extra protection.
