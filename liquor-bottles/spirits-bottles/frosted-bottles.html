Frosted Bottle
 
 

Visually

the frosted finish creates a soft, matte texture that sets them apart from glossy glass containers. This texture gives the bottles an understated elegance, adding a touch of sophistication to their appearance. The deep blue color, combined with the frosted surface, evokes a sense of coolness and mystery, reminiscent of the mountain imagery on the label.

 
 
 

In terms of functionality

the frosted texture provides better grip compared to smooth glass. It reduces the likelihood of slipping from hands, making handling and pouring more convenient. Also, the frosted surface can subtly hide minor smudges or water spots, keeping the bottles looking pristine for longer, which is a practical advantage for display in bars or home collections.

 
 
 

Branding - wise

the frosted background serves as an ideal canvas for the sharp, white mountain design and text. The contrast between the frosted blue and the clear printing makes the brand name other details stand out prominently, enhancing brand recognition while maintaining an overall refined aesthetic.
