Product Feature

Premium Frosted Glass Base: The high-density frosted glass offers a smooth, sophisticated tactile experience while minimizing light transmission, which helps preserve the quality and flavor of beverages.

Durable Silk-Screen Printing: The ink used in our silk-screen process is scratch-resistant and fade-proof, maintaining crisp, vibrant graphics even with long-term storage and handling.

Versatile Visual Expression: The flat, uniform surface of the frosted glass serves as an ideal canvas for silk-screen printing, supporting vivid color reproduction and fine detail rendering for logos and designs.

Seamless Brand Integration: The printing process enables precise alignment of branding elements with the bottle's silhouette, creating a cohesive, professional look that elevates brand perception.

Food-Grade Safety Compliance: All printing materials meet food contact safety standards, ensuring they are non-toxic and suitable for packaging alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages alike.
