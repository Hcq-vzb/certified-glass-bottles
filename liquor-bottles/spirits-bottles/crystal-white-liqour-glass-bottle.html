Crystal White Liqour Glass Bottle

 

It is crafted from premium transparent glass, this elegant bottle showcases the clarity and brilliance of premium spirits. Its pristine crystal-white finish enhances the visual appeal of any liquor, while the smooth, polished surface reflects light beautifully for a luxurious presentation. The durable yet lightweight design ensures safe handling and storage, making it ideal for whiskey, vodka, and other high-end liquors. Customizable with silk-screen printing, hot stamping, or spray coating, it offers brands a sophisticated way to elevate their packaging. With a standardized capacity and stable base, this bottle combines functionality with timeless elegance, perfect for creating standout shelf presence.

FAQ

1. What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? Do you support small batch customisation?

The MOQ for standard products is 6,000 pieces, while customised products are determined based on the complexity of the manufacturing process. We support customers in requesting samples for verification. Special processes such as hot stamping and irregularly shaped bottles require separate assessment, and our engineers will provide you with the most cost-effective solution.

2. Does this 500ml whisky glass bottle comply with international food safety standards?

Yes, all our products are made of food-grade glass and are certified by the FDA, CE and SGS. They do not contain harmful substances such as lead and cadmium. The bottles have undergone rigorous migration testing and fully comply with food contact material standards, making them safe for use in all types of wine packaging.

3. What surface treatment processes does the product support? Are there colour options available?

The product supports a variety of surface treatment processes, such as screen printing, hot stamping, and sandblasting, and can be customised to match Pantone colour charts.
