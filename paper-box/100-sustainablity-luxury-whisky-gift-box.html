The gift box features a precision-engineered interior crafted from high-density molded pulp or custom-cut EVA foam, designed to securely hold the wine bottle in perfect position. This tailored compartmentalization eliminates shifting during transport while adding a refined structural element to the unboxing experience.

For an elevated presentation, some versions incorporate satin-lined cardboard dividers or velvet-covered inserts that combine protection with sophistication. The form-fitting design not only safeguards the bottle from impacts but also enhances visual appeal when opened - turning practical security into a display of thoughtful craftsmanship.

Alternative versions may use thermoformed PET plastic or biodegradable fiber molds, offering both eco-conscious options and premium cushioning. Each material selection balances protective functionality with aesthetic harmony, ensuring your product arrives as impressively as it was packaged.

Production process

 

The creation of our premium whisky gift box begins with precise material selection, including soft-touch laminated paperboard for the exterior and protective EVA foam or molded pulp for interior cushioning. The outer shell is first die-cut into precise dimensions before undergoing specialty printing techniques like UV spot coating or foil stamping for brand embellishments.

Next, artisans assemble the structural components using reinforced glue joints and precision folding techniques to ensure durability.

Quality control includes weight-bearing tests and transit simulations. Finally, each box undergoes hand-finishing - wiping away production residues and applying protective corner guards before being wrapped in anti-scratch film for shipping. The entire process blends automated precision with artisanal detailing to achieve luxury standards.
