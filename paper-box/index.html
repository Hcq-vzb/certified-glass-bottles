Luxury Liquor Box Packaging Manufacturer in China

 

The competition in the high-end spirits market is becoming increasingly fierce, choosing high-end packaging for your spirits can be a great strategic move to enhance brand image and make your brands stand out. As a professional manufacturer and supplier of liquor & spirits box packaging, KIWL also specializes in supplying tailored box packaging solutions for luxury alcohol & spirits brands, our custom premium boxes are designed to elevate the presentation of prestige spirits brands. Crafted with luxurious materials like sustainably sourced wood,specialty paper, rigid board, leather and soft velvet linings, our custom luxury liquor gift boxes offer elegance and security for single bottles of premium whisky, brandy, vodka, tequila, gin and other spirits.

We offer a wide range of liquor & spirits box packaging in different structures, materials and decorations, we could develop your own custom liquor box according to your individual needs. As we have been exporting luxury custom liquor boxes to European & American markets for many years, we have completed numerous projects in the high-end spirits industry, we have rich successful projects and manufacturing experience in prestige spirits packaging. You can select the structures you are interested in from our wide collections of liquor boxes. From structural design to final delivery, we offer end-to-end services to create your exclusive liquor box, you can send us your design elements, ideas, sketches, and then our seasoned designer could make your own unique decoration to show more characters, identities of your brands and convey your brand story.

At our in-house decoration site, we offer a wide range of decoration techniques for surface finishes like elegant matte color, luxury polished color, debossing & embossing logo, hot foil stamping, UV printing, textured paper, we are able to make your ideal blueprints into tangible products that can attract consumers’ attention and present a high-quality brand image. Besides, our liquor box packaging not only considers the content and design of the box, but also protects spirits glass bottles well, we have different styles to make liquor glass bottles fixed in the liquor box including foam or thermoformed inserts, cardboard fixation.
