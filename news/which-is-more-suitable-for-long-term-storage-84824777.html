Which Is More Suitable For Long-term Storage, Aluminum Cover Wine Or Plastic Cover

Feb 19, 2025

Leave a message

Aluminum-capped wine is more suitable for long-term storage than plastic-capped wine.

1. The difference between aluminum-capped wine and plastic-capped wine
Aluminum-capped wine and plastic-capped wine are two common wine bottle caps in our daily life. Aluminum-capped wine is commonly used for high-end wine, white wine, etc., while plastic caps are used for some low-end wine, fruit wine and beer, etc.

The lid of aluminum-capped wine is made of aluminum and sealed with a rubber pad. It has good sealing and preservation effect. After the bottle is sealed, the oxygen in the wine body is difficult to enter the bottle, and the carbon dioxide in the wine body will not leak out, so the quality of the wine can be ensured. The lid of the plastic cap is more transparent and does not have good sealing.

2. Choose aluminum-capped wine for long-term storage
For long-term storage of wine, the reason why aluminum-capped wine is better than plastic cap is that aluminum-capped wine can better guarantee the quality and taste of the wine. Due to the good sealing of aluminum-capped wine, acetaldehyde and other chemicals in the wine bottle are not easy to volatilize, and the gas in the bottle can also be preserved, so the quality and taste of the wine are better.

In addition, if the wine is sealed with a plastic cap for long-term storage, due to the permeability of plastic, the oxygen in the bottle is not easy to be isolated, and the carbon dioxide in the wine is easy to leak out, affecting the quality of the wine.

3. Conclusion
In short, aluminum-capped wine is more suitable for long-term storage than plastic caps. Aluminum-capped wine has good sealing performance, which can better guarantee the quality and taste of the wine, and ensure that the quality of the wine will not deteriorate after long-term storage.
