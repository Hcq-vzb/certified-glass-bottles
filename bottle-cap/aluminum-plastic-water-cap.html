Production Introduction

Aluminum - Plastic Screw Cap: Uses & Advantages

Application

Aluminum - plastic screw caps suit both PET and glass bottles of spring water.

Advantages

Decorative Options

On the top, choices include litho printing, embossing, UV printing, hot foil stamping, and silkscreen printing.

On the sides, four - color offset and silkscreen printing are available.

Liner Variety

We offer different liners like Saranac, sarin, PE liners, and PE liners with QR codes.

Custom Aesthetics

Caps can be customized in color. An easily removable liner can be added to aluminum screw caps for easier consumer opening.

FAQ

 

Q1: Is it possible to request a custom design?
A1: Certainly! Custom - made glass bottles are what we excel at. Our proficient design team can assist you in devising any glass bottle design. Alternately, you can submit your own design draft. The production of a new mold and a sample typically takes around 15 - 20 working days.

 

Q2: How much time does product delivery take?
A2: For new products, the sample delivery period is 20 - 25 working days. For large - scale orders, it's approximately 30 working days after you make the deposit. The precise time varies according to the order quantity.

 

Q3: What information is required for a price quotation?
A3: We need to know the order quantity per batch/annually. Additionally, a detailed drawing specifying materials, color/finishing, capacity, and weight is necessary. These details are crucial for us to provide a quote, and more information will enable us to offer a more precise price.

 

Q4: Can I obtain samples free of charge?
A4: For in - stock items, samples are provided free, yet you'll have to cover the shipping cost. For customized products, there will be a sample charge. Note that we send out numerous samples every month and enjoy significant shipping discounts from carriers such as DHL, FEDEX, TNT, and UPS. I'll seek your approval before sending the sample and send you a picture prior to dispatch.

 

Q5: Do you have a product catalog?
A5: Yes, we can send you our catalog via email.

 

Q6: Do you supply any related products?
A6: Yes, we offer accessories like aluminum screw caps. Moreover, we can simultaneously produce printed cartons which you can reuse after receiving the bottles.

 

Q7: How do you deal with potential problems?
A7: Please take pictures of all relevant aspects. In case of a quality problem, I will replace the faulty items in your subsequent order. If it's not a quality - related issue, I will do everything I can to assist you.
