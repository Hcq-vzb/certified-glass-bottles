Products Features

 

01

Bespoke Craftsmanship Integration

Harnessing the natural grain of premium wood, this stopper leverages laser engraving and silk - screening for deep customization. Laser engraving etches sharp, durable logos, textures, or text onto the wood, while silk - screening applies vivid colors, gradients, or intricate motifs. From mushroom - like shapes to rounded tops, the combination of diverse silhouettes and tailored visuals turns each stopper into a unique brand signature, standing out in luxury packaging.

02

Natural Sealing Excellence

Crafted from high - density solid wood with precision machining, the stopper's structure perfectly fits bottle necks. The wood's innate density, paired with exact dimensional accuracy, creates a "physical lock": it blocks air and moisture, preserving the aroma of fine spirits, the scent of luxury perfumes, or the flavor of gourmet oils. Merging natural material properties with engineering, it safeguards contents while conveying a "pure & elegant" premium vibe-ideal for luxury brands seeking both function and style.

Packing and Transportation
