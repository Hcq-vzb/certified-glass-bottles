Products Features

Material and color

Made of plastic, it is lightweight and comes in a variety of colors. The picture shows colors such as black, yellow, and orange, which are bright and vivid. This not only makes it easy to distinguish different types of oil but also meets the visual needs of brands.

Structural design

It features a pull-ring or flip-top structure (the orange cap on the right can be seen in an open state), which facilitates opening and closing. It allows for better control of the amount of olive oil poured out, and is conducive to sealed storage, reducing contact with air and slowing down the oxidation of the oil.

Compatibility

Such caps usually have standardized size specifications (judging from their appearance, they are likely to fit common olive oil bottle calibers). They can be packaged on automated production lines, adapting to different packaging scenarios, balancing mass production with ease of use, reducing production costs, and improving production efficiency.

Practicality

With a threaded or snap-on design (the texture on the cap surface suggests a similar threaded structure to assist in tightening), it can fit tightly with the bottle mouth, achieving an effective seal. This prevents olive oil from leaking, ensures the stability of the oil during transportation and storage, and allows for smooth repeated opening and closing, meeting daily usage needs.

Packing and Transportation

 

 

 

For packaging, we utilize five-layer corrugated cartons, each lined with an OPP bag to ensure the products stay clean. To avoid friction between layers of caps, separator paper is placed between each layer.

 

When it comes to pallet stacking, we provide plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, customized according to customer needs. Paper corner protectors are installed around the goods, complemented by a top plate, and the entire stack is wrapped with film to offer additional protection.

 

In terms of transportation, we have long-term annual collaborations with international logistics companies, allowing us to deliver high-quality and cost-efficient services. These include express delivery, double customs clearance, sea freight, air freight, as well as various other transportation options.
