Plastic Caps

 

 

Plastic caps are widely used in low-end alcoholic beverages, ready-to-drink beverages and promotional packaging due to their low cost, light weight, corrosion resistance and diverse designs. They occupy an important position in markets with high requirements for cost control and portability.

Features
 

 

Low cost

Plastic caps have low production costs and are suitable for large-scale use.

Lightweight

Plastic caps are lightweight and easy to transport and carry.

Chemical resistance

Plastic materials can resist the acidic substances in alcohol and extend their service life.

Various designs

Plastic caps can be injection molded, with flexible designs to meet different needs.

 

 

Rich colors

Plastic caps are easy to color and provide a variety of color options to enhance product appeal.

Easy to open

Plastic caps are usually designed to be easy to open, which improves user experience.

Environmentally friendly and recyclable

Some plastic caps can be recycled and reused, which is in line with environmental protection trends.
