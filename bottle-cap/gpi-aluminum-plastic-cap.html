Product Introduction:
GPI cap stands for Glass Packaging Institute cap. It is an important component used to seal containers. Made of high-quality materials, it provides a tight seal to protect the contents inside from leakage, contamination, and spoilage. The GPI cap comes in various designs and sizes to fit different bottles.

Products Description

 

 

 

 

 

High - end spirits deserve top - notch GPI closures. Our premium closures are crafted from aluminum - plastic composite paired with a food - grade PE liner. The 180° full - wrap edge design ensures exceptional sealing and long - lasting durability. We offer two sizes: 28mm for narrow - neck bottles, perfect for vodka and some whiskey varieties, and 38mm for wide - neck bottles, great for rum and larger - format spirits. Personalize these closures with printing, embossing, debossing techniques, and choose from a wide range of Pantone colors to match your brand's unique identity.

 

Features & Benefits

 
 

Customization

  • Printing: UV ink resistant to alcohol (50 wipes); 300dpi resolution;
  • Embossing/Debossing: 3D logos/patterns for brand identity;
  • Color Options: Standard gold/silver/black; Pantone matching.

Precision Design

  • Silicone seal adapts to ±0.3mm neck tolerance;
  • Tamper-evident band (break force ≥12N).

Durability & Safety

  • 48-hour salt spray test (ASTM B117) passed;
  • Complies with FDA and EU EC 1935/2004.
