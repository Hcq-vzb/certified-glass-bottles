Our Products

 
 
 

 

 

customized service
 

Laser Engraving

According to your requirements, we can precisely engrave brand logos, patterns, or texts on the top of the wooden cork. The lines are delicate, clear, and long - lasting, endowing the cork with a unique brand mark.

Color Spraying

We offer a wide variety of colors to meet the personalized needs of different brands. Through advanced spraying techniques, the colors are evenly attached, bright, and not easy to fade.

Surface Oil Spraying

To prevent the wood from cracking, we adopt a surface oil - spraying process to form a protective film, effectively enhancing the moisture - resistance and crack - resistance of the wood and extending the service life of the cork.

Bronzing Process

Using the bronzing technique, we can imprint golden patterns or texts on the surface of the cork, presenting a noble and elegant texture and instantly enhancing the product's grade.

Silk - Screen Printing Process

This process can achieve the printing of complex patterns and fine texts with vivid colors and strong adhesion, creating a unique and creative appearance design for the cork.

Adding Iron Blocks

Upon customer request, we can add iron blocks inside the cork to increase its weight and texture, making it more convenient to use and improving the user experience.

 

Product Details

The top of the wooden cork is finely polished, with a smooth surface and comfortable touch, demonstrating excellent craftsmanship.

The main body of the cork has a high degree of fit with the bottle mouth. It adopts a special design to ensure good airtightness, effectively preventing the volatilization and oxidation of the wine and retaining the flavor of the wine to the greatest extent.
