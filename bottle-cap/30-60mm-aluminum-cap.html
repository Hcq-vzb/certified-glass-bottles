Product Features

Superior Sealing Performance

The 30x60mm plain-color aluminum screw caps are produced via high-precision production processes, delivering top-tier sealing capability. The cap body is designed with numerous spiral ridges that form a tight seal on the bottle opening, preventing any liquid from leaking out. Furthermore, the inner plug fits closely against the bottle's mouth, which further strengthens the sealing effect. This excellent sealing ability ensures that packaged products are well-protected during transportation and storage, keeping their quality intact from external disruptions.

Strong Corrosion Resistance

Made of aluminum alloy, these screw caps offer outstanding resistance to corrosion. Even in damp environments, they stay free from rust and corrosion, maintaining their high-quality appearance over time. This trait makes them highly suitable for use in industries such as food and pharmaceuticals-sectors where strict standards for cleanliness and corrosion resistance are a must.

Stable Structure

The 30x60mm plain-color aluminum screw caps feature a stable structural design, remaining resistant to deformation or cracking under regular pressure. During use, they won't age or turn brittle, ensuring consistent performance and appearance for an extended period. This structural stability greatly increases their practical value across a variety of application scenarios.

Easy-to-Use Operation

Tightening and loosening the 30x60mm plain-color aluminum screw caps is simple and intuitive, providing great convenience for users. By gently twisting the cap, users can easily open and close it without needing any additional tools. This user-friendly design helps boost consumer acceptance and satisfaction.

Reusable Design

Crafted from aluminum alloy, these caps can be tightened and loosened repeatedly without getting damaged from frequent use. This makes them highly useful in scenarios that require regular opening and closing-for example, on sauce bottles and beverage containers in the food packaging industry.

Appealing Aesthetics

With a sleek and appealing look, the 30x60mm plain-color aluminum screw caps enhance the overall visual image of the products they're paired with. Through different surface treatment methods (such as glossing or shading) and printing techniques (including embossing and digital printing), they can achieve a wide range of visual effects. These customizable designs help 提升 their competitiveness in the market.

Environmental Sustainability

Aluminum is a sustainable resource. Recycling aluminum uses less than 5% of the energy required to produce primary aluminum, which in turn reduces greenhouse gas emissions. The 30x60mm plain-color aluminum screw caps are easy to recycle, aiding in resource and energy saving while meeting the requirements of environmental protection and sustainable development.

 

In summary, the 30x60mm plain-color aluminum screw caps stand out for their superior sealing, strong corrosion resistance, stable structure, user-friendly operation, reusability, aesthetic appeal, and environmental sustainability. These combined features give them broad application prospects and market demand across multiple industries.

Packing&DElivery

For packaging, we use five - layer corrugated cartons, each lined with an OPP bag to keep the products clean. To prevent abrasion between layers of caps, separator paper is inserted between each layer.

Regarding pallet stacking, we offer plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, tailored to customer requirements. Paper corner protectors are added around the goods, along with a top plate, and the entire stack is secured with wrapping film for extra protection.
