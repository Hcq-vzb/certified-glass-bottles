Products Description

 

 

 

 

 

The 28mm GPI Aluminum-Plastic Composite Cap is a high-performance sealing cap designed specifically for the beverage industry. It is made of high-quality aluminum composite with food-grade plastic, perfectly combining the durability of metal with the sealing properties of plastic. The product is designed with a precision threaded structure that ensures a smooth opening while providing excellent sealing performance, effectively preventing beverage leakage and oxidation, and maintaining the fresh taste of beverages. We strictly follow the food contact material standards to ensure that the product is safe and non-toxic, and can be widely used in mineral water, fruit juice, tea and other types of beverage bottle packaging. Our products support personalized customization service, and we can provide different color choices, logo printing and other surface treatment processes according to customers' needs to meet brand differentiation needs. With reliable sealing performance and exquisite appearance design, this aluminum composite cap has become an ideal choice for beverage manufacturers.

 

 

 

Products Details

 
Production Features
 
 

1. Safety Certifications

This aluminum composite lid is made of food-grade aluminum and high-density plastic composite, which has passed many international food safety certifications. The aluminum outer layer gives the product good structural strength, while the inner layer of food-grade plastic ensures the sealing performance, and the dual material effectively prevents the penetration of external oxygen. All raw materials meet strict food safety standards and are suitable for all kinds of beverage packaging needs.

 

2. Excellent technology

The product adopts a special thread structure design, together with an elastic sealing washer, to provide a reliable sealing effect. After rigorous temperature change tests, it still maintains stable sealing performance in extreme environments. The unique anti-counterfeiting design gives a clear indication when it is first opened, providing additional protection for product safety.

 

3. Wide applicability

They standard specifications are suitable for mainstream beverage bottle mouths, while size adjustment can be provided according to different needs. The product is compatible with all kinds of filling lines and offers a wide range of liner thicknesses to meet the different packaging requirements of aerated and non-aerated beverages. Stable production capacity ensures timely delivery.
