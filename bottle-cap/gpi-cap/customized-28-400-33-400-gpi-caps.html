Product Overview

GPI closures are designed for high-end spirit bottles, featuring aluminum-plastic composite + food-grade PE liner and 180° full-wrap edge for superior sealing and durability. Available in 28mm (narrow-neck) and 38mm (wide-neck), ideal for whiskey, vodka, rum, etc. Customizable with printing, embossing, engraving, and Pantone colors.
