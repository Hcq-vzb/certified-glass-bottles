FAQ

 

 

 

01. What are your delivery lead times and MOQ?

As a professional Chinese packaging supplier, we have a mature production system and efficient supply chain management capabilities, enabling us to provide flexible delivery solutions for B2B clients. Standard products typically have a delivery lead time of 20–30 days, while custom products vary depending on design complexity and order volume. Regarding the minimum order quantity (MOQ), the MOQ for the standard Black GPI Aluminium Plastic Cap is 6,000 units. For long-term partners or large-volume orders, we can offer more flexible MOQ adjustments and priority production scheduling to ensure the stability and efficiency of your supply chain.

02.Do you offer sample testing services? How can I request a sample?

Yes, we support B2B clients in requesting samples for compatibility testing and production line verification. Samples are divided into two types: standard samples and custom samples. Standard samples can be shipped free of charge within 3–5 business days (shipping costs are borne by the client) and are suitable for preliminary evaluation and compatibility testing. For custom samples (e.g., specific colours, printing, or functional structures), we will complete the sample within 5–7 working days after receiving the design draft and charge a sample fee based on the complexity of the sample (this fee can be refunded in subsequent bulk orders). We encourage customers to conduct thorough testing before mass production to ensure perfect compatibility between the product and the production line.

03.How do you ensure that the quality of mass-produced products is consistent with samples?

We have a rigorous quality control system in place, monitoring the entire process from raw material procurement to finished product shipment to ensure that mass-produced products are highly consistent with samples. First, all raw materials are sourced from certified suppliers and undergo incoming inspections (e.g., thickness, hardness, coating uniformity, etc.). Secondly, during production, we combine automated equipment with manual spot checks to monitor the dimensions, sealing performance, print clarity, and surface defects of the lids in real time. Additionally, each batch of products undergoes functional testing before shipment. For our partner clients, we also support third-party factory inspections for product spot checks to ensure that every batch meets your quality requirements.
