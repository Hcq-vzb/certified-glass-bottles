Products Description

33*47mm Aluminum-Plastic Caps

It boasts an aluminum-plastic composite structure, with the outer shell made of aluminum alloy. This aluminum part undergoes careful finishing, resulting in a sleek, uniformly toned surface that feels smooth to the touch. Inside, plastic components fit snugly against the aluminum, forming a tight bond that enhances both the seal and the overall structural strength. These inner plastics are of food-grade quality-typically polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE)-chosen for their reliable sealing ability and resistance to chemical interactions, making them ideal for contact with spirits.
