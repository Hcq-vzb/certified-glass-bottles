Product Overview

 

 

Our GPI caps are painstakingly manufactured for spirit packaging, representing an ideal combination of an unwavering commitment to quality and a deep sense of aesthetics. The design concepts are derived from a precise understanding of global spirit market trends and thorough research into consumer tastes. In the current fiercely competitive spirit market, packaging is not only required to fulfill the fundamental function of safeguarding products but also acts as a crucial medium for brand image, drawing consumers' attention. At present, we provide two popular sizes of GPI lids, namely 28mm and 38mm. Through extensive testing and refinement, these two sizes can perfectly fit the various common glass bottles for spirits available in the market. Whether it's a modern - designed bottle with a sleek and stylish appearance or a traditionally - shaped glass bottle exuding a vintage and refined charm, our lids can connect smoothly and fit snugly, catering to your diverse packaging demands. Whether it's for large - scale production by major distilleries or customized orders for small artisanal wineries, our GPI lids can be your optimal packaging choice, enabling your products to stand out among the competition on store shelves.

Company Profile

Shandong KIWL Co.,LTD

Jiangsu KIWL Machinery Manufacturing Group Co., Ltd. was established by a consortium of plant owners and manufacturers in the packaging industry. This consortium encompasses glass bottle factories, closure manufacturing plants, twist - off cap producers, label manufacturers, bottling machine and cap - making machine suppliers, as well as other partner factories specializing in liquor promotion items. The group's objective is to create a genuine one - stop packaging solution platform. The key benefit of our group operation lies in uniting various leading plants across different packaging sectors into a cohesive entity. This working mechanism inherently eliminates vicious competition, enabling each member to focus on collaborative efforts towards a common goal: offering professional packaging solutions and forging stable, long - term business partnerships with clients. Our team possesses 15 years of industry experience, and our products have been exported to over 30 countries worldwide. Our team's mission is to become a professional solution provider, leveraging the systematic resources and expertise that have been accumulated through our extensive, long - term practical experience.
