Product Details

 
 
product key technologies
 
 
01.

Material

This 28mm Sacmi carbonated beverage bottle cap is made of high-quality aluminium, which is lighter than traditional iron caps and has excellent rust and corrosion resistance. The orange coating on the surface is not only aesthetically pleasing, but also enhances scratch resistance, improving the overall texture and durability of the product, making it suitable for long-term storage and transportation.

 
 
02.

Sealing Performance

Built-in TPE gasket with excellent elasticity and sealing performance, effectively withstands high pressure inside carbonated beverage bottles, prevents gas leakage and oxidation of contents, ensures beverage taste and freshness, and provides reliable quality assurance for the product.

 
 
 
03.

Functionality

The cap design features micro-ventilation holes that quickly release internal pressure when the bottle is opened, preventing liquid from splashing and enhancing the user experience. The cap is compatible with high-speed filling production lines, ensuring production efficiency and stability to meet the needs of large-scale industrial production.

 
 
04.

Environmental

Aluminium is 100% recyclable, in line with modern green packaging trends, helping brands achieve their sustainability goals. Its environmental friendliness not only meets policy and regulatory requirements, but also enhances the brand's social responsibility and market competitiveness.
