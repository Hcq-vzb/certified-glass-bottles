Product Details

 

 

Structure and Materials

This 3060mm aluminium cap is made from food-grade high-quality aluminium, with a diameter of 30mm and a height of 60mm. It features a sturdy yet lightweight structure. Aluminium offers excellent ductility and corrosion resistance, effectively protecting the contents of the bottle from external environmental influences. The surface undergoes precise polishing and coating treatment, presenting a lustrous black finish with a smooth and delicate texture, exuding a premium quality feel. The inner sarantin gasket of the bottle cap fits tightly against the bottle opening, ensuring it remains deformation-free and rust-free even with long-term use.

Performance

This aluminium cap adopts a spiral sealing structure, offering excellent sealing performance to effectively block air ingress, prevent oxidation and spoilage of the liquid, and extend the product's shelf life. Its tightly designed seal ring provides strong leak-proof performance, maintaining stability even during transportation or storage. Additionally, the cap features moisture-proof and dust-proof functions, further ensuring the quality and safety of the contents.

Design and Value

Externally, this aluminium cap features a predominantly black colour scheme, complemented by the brand logo and customised patterns. The overall design is sleek yet elegant, with a strong visual impact. The exquisite printing techniques and pattern design not only enhance brand recognition but also convey the product's high-quality positioning, helping the brand establish a differentiated competitive advantage in the market and boost consumer purchasing desire.

Application Scenarios

This aluminium cap is widely applicable for packaging bottled products such as wine, fruit wine, spirits, and premium condiments. Its exceptional sealing performance and aesthetic design make it the preferred accessory for mid-to-high-end beverage brands. This aluminium cap not only meets current market demands but also possesses significant market potential, serving as a crucial tool for brands to enhance product value and expand market share.

 

 

 

 

Process & Design

 

 

 

 

 

 

1. Conceptualize the bottle cap shape: Based on functional requirements and brand identity, generate creative ideas and sketches to establish a clear direction for the design.
2. 3D modeling: Utilize modeling software to transform design concepts into precise 3D models, visualizing dimensions, structure, and details for further development.
3. Render visualizations: Apply rendering techniques to produce realistic images, simulating different materials and lighting conditions to support design refinement.
4. Create handmade prototypes: Manually craft initial samples to test tactile feel and structural integrity, providing a tangible reference for mass production.

5. Confirm mold data: Compile and verify essential technical specifications for mold fabrication to ensure dimensional accuracy and consistency in large-scale manufacturing.
6. Design brand logo integration: Incorporate the brand logo or other elements into the cap design to enhance visual appeal and strengthen brand recognition.
7. Final product completion: After multiple rounds of testing and optimization, the finished product meets all functional and aesthetic standards and is ready for market launch.
