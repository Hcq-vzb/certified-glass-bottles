Product Details

 
01.

Gold Stamping

This process involves applying a metallic foil, usually gold or silver, to the surface of the aluminum cap using heat and pressure. A custom-engraved mold is heated and pressed against the foil, transferring the metallic layer onto the cap in precise patterns or text. This creates a luxurious, reflective finish, often used for branding, logos, or decorative elements. Gold stamping is highly durable, resistant to fading, and adds a premium look to the caps, making them ideal for high-end products such as liquors, perfumes, and pharmaceuticals.

 
02.

Non Refillable

The Non Refillable function refers to a feature achieved through special structural design, which makes the container unable to be properly resealed or refilled with contents after the first opening. It is particularly suitable for products such as spirits that require anti-counterfeiting measures to prevent secondary refilling and counterfeiting. When the user opens the bottle cap for the first time, the key components will suffer permanent damage or displacement. Even if liquid is forced to be poured in, leakage will occur due to seal failure, and the original intact state cannot be restored. This effectively eliminates the problem of counterfeit and shoddy products being produced by refilling recycled empty bottles, thus protecting brand reputation and consumers' rights and interests.
