Product Details

 
 
01.

Material and structure

This bottle cap is made of high-quality aluminium, which is lightweight yet sturdy, with excellent corrosion resistance and pressure resistance. The cap is equipped with a Sarantin gasket, which provides excellent sealing performance to prevent air and moisture from entering. The overall structure is precise and fits 31.5mm diameter wine bottles, ensuring secure sealing that is not easily deformed or loosened, making it an ideal packaging solution for alcoholic beverages.

 
 
02.

Sealing performance

Sarantin gaskets have excellent elasticity and sealing properties, effectively blocking oxygen and preventing wine from oxidising and deteriorating. The aluminium cap fits tightly onto the bottle neck, ensuring the stability of the wine's flavour during long-term storage. This sealing design is particularly suitable for still wines and sparkling wines, maintaining the pressure inside the bottle and preventing air leakage or seepage, thus preserving the quality of the wine.

 
 
 
03.

Applicable scenarios

This aluminium cap is specially designed for wine bottles and is widely used in winery bottling lines, winery customisation, and home bottling needs. Whether it is still wine, sweet wine, or slightly sparkling wine, it can provide professional-grade bottling effects. Its standardised size also facilitates the operation of automated bottling equipment, improving production efficiency and meeting the different needs of commercial and individual users.

 
 
 
04.

Appearance and brand value

The aluminium cap has a smooth surface that can be printed in a variety of colours or embossed to enhance brand recognition and product quality. The lightweight metallic texture gives the bottle a modern and refined look, making it suitable for the high-end wine market. Customised designs not only enhance the aesthetic appeal of the packaging, but also effectively convey the brand image, improving consumers' first impressions of the product and their desire to purchase it.

FAQ

01.What types of wine is this aluminium cap suitable for?

This aluminium cap is designed for wine bottles with a 31.544 mm neck diameter and is suitable for a wide range of wines, including still wines, sweet wines and lightly sparkling wines. Its Sarantin gasket provides excellent sealing properties, effectively preventing oxidation and maintaining the stability of the wine's flavour. It is an ideal choice for bottling at wineries, sealing at vineyards and home storage.

02.What is the function of the Sarantin liner?

The Sarantin liner has excellent elasticity and sealing performance, tightly fitting the bottle opening to effectively isolate air and moisture, preventing oxidation or leakage of the wine. It also maintains the pressure inside the bottle, making it particularly suitable for wines that require a stable storage environment, ensuring that the wine quality remains unchanged even after long-term storage.

03.Can the aluminium cap be customised with a brand logo or colour?

Yes. This aluminium cap supports customised printing, embossing and a variety of colour treatments. Logos, text or patterns can be printed according to brand requirements to enhance product recognition and packaging quality. The customisation service is suitable for winery brand promotion, enhancing market competitiveness while meeting the aesthetic needs of high-end consumer groups.
