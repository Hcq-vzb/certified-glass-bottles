Jiangsu KIWL Machinery Manufacturing Group Co., Ltd is one of the most professional manufacturers and suppliers of 38mm aluminum cap in China. Please rest assured to wholesale high quality 38mm aluminum cap from our factory. Contact us for more details.

 

Our 38mm Roll-On Pilfer Proof (ROPP) Caps are pioneers in the packaging industry, providing a durable and secure seal for glass and plastic bottles alike. This cap is ideally suited for sealing a variety of products such as spirits, wines, oils, sauces, and juices, ensuring the safety of your goods during transportation and storage.

 

A key feature of our ROPP caps is their high-quality construction. Made from aluminum, these caps offer exceptional corrosion resistance, rust prevention, and wear resistance. As such, they are highly suitable for use in multiple industries including food, beverages, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.

 

Another benefit of using our ROPP caps is their tamper-evident design. The cap's groove guideline makes it easy to identify if the product has been opened or tampered with, thereby ensuring product safety and authenticity. This feature is particularly important for companies selling to consumers who prioritize product quality and safety.

 

The 38mm ROPP cap is also an eco-friendly choice for packaging products. These caps are recyclable, reducing the demand for non-renewable materials and minimizing waste in the environment. This makes our ROPP caps an excellent option for businesses focused on sustainable operations and minimizing environmental impact.

 

Our ROPP caps are also compatible with a variety of sealing equipment, allowing businesses to easily integrate them into their existing packaging systems. The cap's tight sealing mechanism ensures product freshness, with its airtight design protecting the contents from contamination and oxidation.

 

The 38mm ROPP cap comes in a range of colors including gold, silver, black, or white, allowing you to customize your packaging and create a unique brand image. Additionally, the cap's surface can be customized with your company's logo or other designs, creating a unique and professional look for your brand.

We value efficiency and reliability, ensuring our customers receive their orders in optimal condition and on time. Our ROPP caps are no exception, offering a durable, practical, and eco-friendly packaging solution of the highest quality.

 

In conclusion, our 38mm ROPP caps are the ideal choice for businesses seeking high-quality and efficient packaging solutions. The 38mm aluminum ROPP cap is specifically designed for beverage packaging and fits the standard GPI 1650 bottle neck in the US.

 

The compression-molded liner and internal coating materials, including the external coating, comply with FDA standards and can withstand temperatures of up to 121°C for 15 minutes. Additionally, the liner material is ozone-resistant, making it suitable for sealing high-quality mineral water. The aluminum cap is made of 0.23mm thick 3105 aluminum alloy.

 

Produced on Sacmi high-speed machines imported from Italy, the 38mm aluminum ROPP caps have a production capacity of 60,000 pieces per hour and are equipped with automatic detection equipment. Generally, our production lead time is 2 weeks.
