Aluminium Caps

 

 

Our Aluminum caps are widely used in various types of wine bottles due to their sealing, corrosion resistance, lightness and aesthetics, such as wine, spirits, fruit wine and sake, and custom wine, especially in the high-end wine market. 

Features
 

 

Strong sealing

Aluminum caps can effectively isolate air and moisture, prevent wine from oxidation and volatilization, and maintain the flavor and quality of wine.

Corrosion resistance

Aluminum has good corrosion resistance, can resist acidic substances in wine, and extend service life.

Lightweight and easy to open

Aluminum caps are light in weight and easy to open. Some designs also provide anti-counterfeiting functions to enhance user experience.

Strong plasticity

Aluminum is easy to process and can be made into various shapes and sizes to meet the needs of different wine bottles.

Environmentally friendly and recyclable

Aluminum caps can be recycled and reused, meeting environmental protection requirements.

Beautiful and diverse

The surface of the aluminum cap can be processed in a variety of ways, such as brushing, polishing, printing, etc., to enhance the appearance of the product.
