Product Description

 

 

28mm Roll-top Bottle Caps

This rolled-edge cap is made of high-quality aluminium, with a smooth surface and uniform colour, offering a lightweight yet durable feel. It features a food-grade PE gasket inside, providing excellent sealing performance to effectively prevent liquid leakage and external contamination, ensuring the freshness of beverages. The rolled-edge design makes it easy to open and close, and it is compatible with various sizes of beverage bottles. Whether it's carbonated drinks, juices, or mineral water, it can be easily matched, making it an ideal choice for beverage packaging, combining practicality and reliability.
