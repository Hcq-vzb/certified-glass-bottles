Products Description

 

 

 

 

 

 

33×47mm Aluminum Bottle Caps with Dual Liner Options These premium 33×47mm aluminum caps feature precision engineering for luxury beverage packaging. Crafted from high-grade aluminum, they offer superior corrosion resistance and customizable metallic finishes in gold, silver or colored variants. The caps come with two sealing options: standard oxygen-barrier liners for reliable preservation, or glass ball inserts designed specifically for controlled pouring of spirits. The glass ball version creates a distinctive "click" sound upon opening, adding sensory appeal to premium liquor packaging. Manufactured in ISO-certified facilities, these tamper-evident caps meet international safety standards while allowing for custom embossing and anti-counterfeit features. Ideal for whiskey, vodka and high-end liqueurs, the 33×47mm size ensures perfect compatibility with premium bottles. The aluminum construction guarantees durability while maintaining elegant aesthetics, making these caps the perfect choice for brands seeking to elevate their packaging with functional sophistication.

Production Features
 
 

1. Material and Appearance Design

This 3347mm aluminum bottle cap is crafted from high-grade aluminum, offering excellent corrosion resistance and structural strength. 2.Sealing Liner Options and Functional Features

4.Application Scenarios and Brand Value
