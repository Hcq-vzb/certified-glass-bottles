FAQ

01.How effective is this bottle stopper at sealing? Can it prevent wine or liquids from evaporating?

This bottle stopper is made of high-molecular material, which has excellent elasticity and sealing performance. It can effectively isolate air and prevent the liquid in the bottle from evaporating or oxidising. Whether you are storing red wine, white wine, cooking oil or homemade drinks, it can effectively preserve the original flavour and freshness, making it an ideal choice for home storage and commercial display.

02.Are wood and polymer materials safe? Will they affect the taste of the liquid inside the bottle?

Yes, the wood and polymer materials used in this bottle stopper are food-grade safe materials. They are odourless, non-toxic, and will not react chemically with the liquid inside the bottle or affect its flavour. The wooden part has undergone environmentally friendly treatment, and the polymer stopper is stable and durable, making it safe for use in sealing and storing all kinds of food and beverages.

03.What is special about the T-shaped design? Is it easy to insert and remove?

The T-shaped design is not only aesthetically pleasing, but also easy to grip with your fingers, making insertion and removal easier and more effortless. The wooden top provides good friction, allowing for stable operation even with wet hands. At the same time, this design enhances the overall structural strength, making the bottle cap both practical and decorative, ideal for frequent opening and closing.
