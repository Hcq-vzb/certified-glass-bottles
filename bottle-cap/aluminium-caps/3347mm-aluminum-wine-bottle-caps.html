Product Details

 
 
01.

1.exquisite craftsmanship

This bottle cap is crafted from high-purity aluminum, formed through multiple precision molding processes to exhibit outstanding toughness and anti-aging properties. The base material has received authoritative SGS certification, fully complying with EU food contact material regulations and eliminating the risk of any toxic substance migration. A unique continuous annealing process renders the aluminum's internal structure more uniform and stable, which not only ensures the longevity of the final product but also creates ideal conditions for subsequent decorative processes.

 
 
02.

2. Top Protection System

The cap's structure features an ergonomic design, complemented by micron-level precision machining, to achieve a flawless, seamless fit with the container's mouth. Its innovative triple-seal technology establishes a three-dimensional protective barrier, completely blocking the ingress of external gases and moisture. Rigorously verified by an independent third-party laboratory, its airtightness performance has reached an industry-leading benchmark, significantly extending the beverage's optimal flavor window. It is particularly suited for reserve wines requiring aging, providing robust protection for the product's intrinsic value.

 
 
 
03.

Enriched Decoration Processes

The cap offers diverse customization choices: Ombré Coating for smooth color transitions and fade-resistant hues; Metallization Plating for a luxurious gold or silver finish; and Laser Etching for ultra-fine, intricate detailing.

Each decorative technique undergoes rigorous durability testing to guarantee scratch and corrosion resistance, maintaining pristine appearance over time. Brands can freely mix and match finishes based on their market strategy, crafting distinctive packaging that elevates visual appeal and consumer recognition.

 
 
 
04.

Exclusive customization solutions

We offer end-to-end customization, from initial concept to final production, including brand engraving, unique mold design, and non-standard sizing. With expert design consultants and automated manufacturing systems, we swiftly adapt to diverse customization requests.

From 3D prototyping to mass production, every stage is overseen by dedicated specialists. Whether for craft brewers or global beverage brands, we deliver tailored packaging solutions that enhance brand identity and create a competitive edge in the market.

 

 


Process & Design

 

 

 

 

1. Design the bottle cap: The bottle cap's initial design is created through sketches, balancing ergonomics, visual appeal, and brand alignment to ensure market competitiveness.
2. Modeling: Advanced CAD software converts sketches into a precise 3D model, enabling dimensional fine-tuning and virtual testing before prototyping.​​​​​​​
3. Rendering: High-resolution rendering simulates material finishes and lighting effects, revealing design refinements needed for optimal appearance.​​​​​​​
4. Handboard of Printing: A 3D-printed prototype validates the cap's tactile experience, ergonomics, and structural integrity through hands-on evaluation.

5. Confirmation of cutting die data for bottle caps: Precision mold specifications are locked in, ensuring mass-produced caps meet exact design tolerances and functional requirements.​​​​​​​
6. Bottle cap logo design: Custom logos or embossing are incorporated, enhancing shelf impact while maintaining the cap's technical performance.​​​​​​​
7. Final Product: After iterative testing, the finalized cap enters production, delivering seamless functionality and distinctive branding to end users
