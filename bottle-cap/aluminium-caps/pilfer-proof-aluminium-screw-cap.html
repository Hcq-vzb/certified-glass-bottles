Product Characteristics
 
 

Precise Dimensions

The carefully designed 31.5×44mm size perfectly fits various spirits glass bottle shapes. Whether it is a small and delicate mini - bottle or a large and magnificent large - capacity bottle, it can fit closely, ensuring the integrity and coordination of the packaging.

 

Material and Sealing

The main body is made of high - quality aluminum, which is sturdy, durable, and has excellent corrosion - resistance, effectively protecting the spirits from the external environment. An EPE gasket is equipped inside. The EPE material has good elasticity and cushioning performance, which can closely fill the gap between the aluminum cap and the bottle mouth, forming an excellent sealing effect, minimizing the volatilization and leakage of the spirits, and ensuring that every drop of spirits can maintain its original flavor and quality.
