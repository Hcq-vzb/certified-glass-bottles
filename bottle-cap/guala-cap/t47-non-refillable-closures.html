The 33mm x 47mm aluminum-plastic non-refillable closure is a tamper-evident and anti-refill safety cap designed for bottles. It consists of an aluminum outer shell and an inner plastic liner, ensuring durability and a secure seal. The non-refillable mechanism prevents unauthorized refilling, enhancing product safety and preventing tampering. Its tamper-evident feature provides visible proof of opening, ensuring consumer trust. Widely used in pharmaceutical, chemical, and beverage industries, it offers excellent leak-proof performance, compliance with safety standards, and protection against counterfeiting. The compact size and reliable design make it ideal for high-security packaging needs.

 

Gold stamping and roller printing are two advanced decorative techniques used in the production of aluminum caps, enhancing their aesthetic appeal and functionality.

**Gold Stamping**: This process involves applying a metallic foil, typically gold or silver, onto the aluminum cap surface using heat and pressure. A custom-designed die is heated and pressed onto the foil, transferring the metallic layer onto the cap in precise patterns or text. This creates a luxurious, reflective finish that is often used for branding, logos, or decorative elements. Gold stamping is highly durable, resistant to fading, and adds a premium look to the caps, making them ideal for high-end products like spirits, perfumes, and pharmaceuticals.

**Roller Printing**: Roller printing is a high-speed, high-precision method used to apply intricate designs, text, or patterns onto aluminum caps. It involves a series of rollers, each engraved with specific parts of the design. As the caps pass through the rollers, ink is transferred onto their surface in layers, creating detailed and vibrant images. This technique allows for multi-color printing and fine details, ensuring consistency across large production runs. Roller printing is cost-effective, versatile, and widely used for branding, product information, and decorative purposes.

Both techniques enhance the visual appeal and marketability of aluminum caps while maintaining their functional integrity.
