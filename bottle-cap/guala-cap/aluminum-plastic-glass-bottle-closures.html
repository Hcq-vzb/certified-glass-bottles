Functions of the Plastic Inner Plug
 

Double Sealing

The inner plug is made of highly elastic PE plastic pads, which can fit tightly to the bottle mouth to form a double - sealing structure, effectively preventing oxidation and leakage, and ensuring that the wine always remains fresh and of high quality.

 

Anti - counterfeiting and Anti - theft

The inner plug is designed as a one - time breakable structure with a tensile force ≥ 15N, and is equipped with laser coding, which can effectively prevent secondary filling and protect the safety of products and the reputation of the brand.

 

Quiet Opening and Closing

During the opening and closing process, the inner plug can reduce the friction noise between metal and glass, bringing a more comfortable user experience.
