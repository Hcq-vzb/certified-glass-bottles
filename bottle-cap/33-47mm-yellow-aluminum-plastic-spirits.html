Products Description

 

 

33*47mm Yellow Aluminum-Plastic Bottle Cap

 

This 33×47mm yellow aluminum - plastic bottle cap has a composite structure. The outer aluminum alloy is finely processed, showing a smooth, uniformly golden surface with textured patterns and embossed "NOBEL" lettering on the side. The inner layer is food - grade plastic like PP or PE, tightly integrated with the aluminum to boost sealing and reinforcement. An orange emblem adorns the top, adding a unique design touch. It combines aluminum's sleek look and plastic's sealing ability, ideal for high - end packaging.
