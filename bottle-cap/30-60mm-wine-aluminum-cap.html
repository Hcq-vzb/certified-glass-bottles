Packing and Transportation

 

 

 

For packaging, we utilize five-layer corrugated cartons, each lined with an OPP bag to ensure the products stay clean. To avoid friction between layers of caps, separator paper is placed between each layer.

 

When it comes to pallet stacking, we provide plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, customized according to customer needs. Paper corner protectors are installed around the goods, complemented by a top plate, and the entire stack is wrapped with film to offer additional protection.

 

In terms of transportation, we have long-term annual collaborations with international logistics companies, allowing us to deliver high-quality and cost-efficient services. FAQ

01.Is the 3060 wine cap compatible with all types of wine bottles?

The 3060 stopper is designed to fit most standard wine bottle models, including Bordeaux, Burgundy, and Champagne bottles, and works with common capacities such as 375 ml and 750 ml. 

02.What advantages does this type of wine cap have compared to traditional corks?

Compared to traditional corks, new-style wine caps like the 30*60mm screw cap offer more stable sealing performance, which can reduce the probability of cork taint. Additionally, screw caps are easy to open without the need for a corkscrew, and there are no issues such as cork breakage.

03.Do the materials of the wine cap affect the taste of the wine?

No. The material will not react with the wine, nor release strange odors, thus not altering the taste of the wine.

04.Can it be customized?

Yes. And we can customize the techniques and patterns according to the customers' requirements.
