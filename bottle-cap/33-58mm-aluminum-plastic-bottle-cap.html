33*58mm Aluminum-Plastic Bottle Cap

Product Introduction:
It presents an aluminum-plastic combined structure, with the outer part made of aluminum alloy and the inner part consisting of plastic components. The aluminum alloy part undergoes fine processing, featuring a smooth surface and uniform color. The plastic part is tightly integrated with the aluminum alloy, playing a role in sealing and reinforcement. The inner plastic part generally uses food-grade plastics such as polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE), which have excellent sealing properties and chemical resistance.
