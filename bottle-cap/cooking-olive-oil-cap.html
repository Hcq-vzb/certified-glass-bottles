Products Description

Materials and Features

High-Grade Aluminum

Aluminum olive oil caps are typically made of premium aluminum. This material is well-known for its excellent sealing performance, moisture resistance, and anti-oxidation properties. Despite being lightweight, aluminum is strong enough to effectively resist environmental corrosion, protecting the quality and safety of olive oil.

Appealing Visual Design

These caps are not only functional but also visually attractive. Their metallic luster and high-gloss finish bring an elegant, modern touch to olive oil containers, greatly enhancing the overall visual appeal of the product.

Functions and Advantages

Excellent Sealing Performance

Aluminum olive oil caps feature threads that fit tightly onto bottle necks, creating a reliable seal. This design acts as a barrier against air and light penetration, thereby extending the shelf life of olive oil and preserving its original flavor and taste.

Spill-Proof Structure

Some aluminum olive oil caps come with spill-proof components such as pouring spouts and stoppers. This makes pouring olive oil more convenient while preventing oil from splashing out, reducing waste and the risk of contamination.

Eco-Friendly and Recyclable

Made from recyclable materials, aluminum olive oil caps meet environmental requirements. After use, they can be recycled and reintroduced into production cycles, minimizing environmental pollution and conserving resources.

Packing and Delivery

For packaging, we use five - layer corrugated cartons, each lined with an OPP bag to keep the products clean. To prevent abrasion between layers of caps, separator paper is inserted between each layer.

Regarding pallet stacking, we offer plywood or solid wood pallets with fumigation certificates, tailored to customer requirements. Paper corner protectors are added around the goods, along with a top plate, and the entire stack is secured with wrapping film for extra protection.

In terms of transportation, we maintain annual partnerships with international logistics agencies, enabling us to provide high - quality and cost - effective services. These include express shipping, double customs clearance, sea freight, air freight, and multiples transportation solutions.
