Products Description

 

 

Luxury Black Aluminum-plastic Olive Oil Caps

This olive oil cap harmonizes premium aesthetics with intelligent functionality. The outer shell is a sleek black aluminum, finished with a polished coating and adorned with delicate gold detailing - a design choice that radiates elegance, perfectly aligning with high - end olive oil branding. Textured ridges encircling the edge ensure a secure grip, enabling effortless opening. Inside, food - grade white plastic stoppers take center stage. Their innovative structure forms an airtight seal when pressed into the bottle neck, effectively blocking oxygen to maintain the oil's freshness. During pouring, the stoppers' precisely engineered channels act as flow controllers, preventing drips and ensuring accurate dispensing.

Products Features

Enhanced Sealing for Freshness

The white plastic pour spout, featuring ridged rings, forms a tight dual - seal when combined with the threaded black cap. It blocks air, moisture, and impurities from entering the bottle, slowing oxidation to preserve the olive oil's delicate flavor, antioxidant - rich nutrients (e.g., oleuropein), and preventing premature rancidity.

Precise Pouring Control

The cross - shaped flow guide inside the cap is a standout feature. When pouring, oil flows evenly through the cross gaps, avoiding messy spills. Users can also adjust the pouring speed by tilting the bottle: a slight tilt allows slow, controlled pouring, while a steeper angle enables faster dispensing. This design supports precise portioning, reducing waste and improving the user experience.
