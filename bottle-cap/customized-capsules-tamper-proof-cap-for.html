Products Description

Our aluminum-plastic combined capsules are designed for premium wine, spirits, and liquor packaging, combining the durability of aluminum with the flexibility of plastic. They feature a tamper-evident tear strip to ensure product integrity and prevent unauthorized opening, which is essential for brand security and consumer trust.

Available in a wide range of colors (custom Pantone colors available) and finishes-matte, glossy, or metallic-these capsules can be customized with your logo via hot stamping, UV printing, or embossing. They fit standard bottle necks (including 30x60mm, 31.5x60mm sizes) and are compatible with both automatic and manual capping machines.
