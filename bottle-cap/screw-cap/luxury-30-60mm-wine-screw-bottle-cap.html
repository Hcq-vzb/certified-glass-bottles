Customization Services

 

Color Customization

A wide range of colors is available. Whether it's the classic gold and silver, or the personalized wine red, royal blue, etc., exclusive colors can be customized according to your brand tone and product style, creating a unique visual identity.

Embossing Customization

Through advanced technology, various embossed patterns and texts, such as brand logos, winery emblems, vintage numbers, etc., can be made on the wine aluminum caps, adding a sense of three - dimensionality and texture, and highlighting the high - end quality of the product.

Foil Stamping Customization

Product Details
 

 

Edge Treatment

The edges of the wine aluminum caps are finely polished, smooth and round. This not only avoids scratching the user when opening but also demonstrates exquisite craftsmanship.

Internal Gasket

A high - quality gasket is built - in, with excellent flexibility and sealing performance. It can closely fit the bottle mouth, effectively preventing wine from volatilizing and air from entering, and maintaining the flavor and quality of the wine.

Surface Texture

The surface of the aluminum cap is designed with unique textures, which not only increases friction for easy opening but also adds a unique aesthetic to the product.
