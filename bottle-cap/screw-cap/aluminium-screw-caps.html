Wine aluminum screwcaps usually use two different material liner, Sarantin liner and Saranex liner respectively. There are difference in cost and airtightness between of them, Sarantin due to good oxygen permeability can keep wine safe for 10 years, Saranex liner last three year in sealing, the cost of Sarantin liner is higher than that of Saranex liner accordingly, Sarantin liner is very popular in Austraila and New Zealand Market. Our Sarantin liner are imported from German Meyer and France MJG company, and including inner coating are met to FDA standard.

 

The thickness and material of aluminum screwcaps is 0.23mm, 8011 aluminum alloy. Aluminum wine bottle screw top is produced by high speed BORTOLIINKEMO machine that was imported from Europe, 40000pcs per hour, equipped with automatic inspection machine in order to control quality.

 

For 30x60mm aluminum screwcaps, it is professionally fit to GPI 1680 BVS bottle finish that is American Glass Packaging Institute.

 

The six different print color could be printed on both top and side of wine aluminum screwcaps by Italian CALF and OMSO multicolor printer. Swiss MADAG top positioned hot foil stamping machine could print design on the top and sides. Besides, top embossing and flexographic printing can be also workable in mass production.
