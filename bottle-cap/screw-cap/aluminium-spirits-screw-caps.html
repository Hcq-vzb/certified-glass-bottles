For aluminum spirits screw caps, there are many popular size in spirits industry, such as 28x16mm/30x35mm/30x44mm/30x50mm/31.5x44mm with PE liner or non-refillable plastic pourer.

 

The thickness and material of aluminum screw caps is 0.23mm, 8011 aluminum alloy. Aluminum spirits screw caps are produced by high speed BORTOLIINKEMO machine that was imported from Europe, 40000pcs per hour, equipped with automatic inspection machine.

 

The six different print color could be printed on both top and side of screw caps by Italian CALF and OMSO multicolor printer. Swiss MADAG top positioned hot foil stamping machine could print design on the top and sides. Besides, top embossing and Flexographic closure can be also workable in the production.

 

For new clients, the order could be started from 50000pcs with each item. Generally speaking, our production lead time is 3~4 weeks. Before mass production, we would like to send you samples for your testing in order to control risk.
