Product Introduction

 

The aluminum-plastic cap, which measures 33mm by 47mm, is an aluminum-plastic composite one created through the lamination of aluminum foil and plastic film using specialized techniques. It functions as a sealing packaging material.

The aluminum-plastic cap features a one-time seal design, ensuring great convenience and ease of use. With a simple twist, users can effortlessly open the cap, gaining quick access to the product contained within.

Product Features

 

Superior airtightness: The aluminum foil component of the aluminum-plastic cap boasts exceptional airtight capabilities, efficiently blocking gas exchange between the package's interior and exterior, thus preserving the product's freshness and quality.

 

Outstanding moisture barrier: The plastic layer of the aluminum-plastic cap efficiently guards against moisture infiltration, safeguarding the packaged product from the detrimental effects of moisture and oxidation.

 

Strong corrosion resistance: The aluminum-plastic cap is compatible with packaging a wide range of substances with diverse chemical properties, exhibiting robust resistance to acids, bases, corrosion, and other factors.

 

Attractive appearance: Aluminum-plastic caps are available in a diverse array of colors and can undergo special surface treatments, like high gloss or matte finishes, to cater to the packaging requirements of various products.
