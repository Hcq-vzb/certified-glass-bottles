Packaging method

The packaging method of 30*60mm Plain Color Aluminum Screw Caps is mainly selected according to their size, weight and transportation requirements. The following are some common packaging methods:

Carton Packing

Given that 30x60mm Plain Color Aluminum Screw Caps are small in size and light in weight, cartons represent a perfect packaging alternative. Cartons are inexpensive and environmentally conscious due to their recyclability. They have the ability to protect the bottle caps from slight bumps and scratches. When packing, these 30x60mm Plain Color Aluminum Screw Caps ought to be orderly placed inside the carton. Furthermore, materials such as foam or bubble wrap can be utilized to firmly hold and protect the caps, thus avoiding any shifting or crashing during transportation.

01

Plastic Bag Packaging

For 30x60mm Plain Color Aluminum Screw Caps that require enhanced protection, especially those that are larger or heavier in size, plastic bags prove to be a viable option. Typically, plastic bags feature remarkable durability and reliable sealing capabilities. These qualities enable them to effectively safeguard the bottle caps from potential damage during transit. During the packing process, it is crucial to make sure that the caps are securely placed inside the plastic bag. Additionally, suitable cushioning materials should be employed to provide extra protection for the caps.

02

Pallet packaging

When 30x60mm Plain Color Aluminum Screw Caps are produced on a large scale, pallet packaging is utilized. Pallets are engineered for mechanical loading, unloading, and transportation, and have the capacity to support substantial weights. During the packing process, these 30x60mm Plain Color Aluminum Screw Caps can be orderly stacked on the pallet and subsequently secured with straps or ropes. This method guarantees that the bottle caps stay undamaged and do not become dislodged during transit, while also enabling efficient mechanical handling.
