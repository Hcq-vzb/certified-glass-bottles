Products Description

 

 

 

 

We are proud to introduce our newly designed 3347mm and 3358mm aluminum-plastic Guala caps, specially crafted for high-end liquor packaging. These caps combine the durability of aluminum with the flexibility of plastic, ensuring both tight seal integrity and a unique visual appeal and tactile experience for your liquor brands. Through exquisite manufacturing processes, we provide practical yet aesthetically pleasing packaging solutions for your premium liquor products.

Material Composition

  1. Made from high-quality aluminum and eco-friendly plastic composite, balancing strength and weight reduction.
