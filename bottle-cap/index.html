Bottle Cap Types

 

KIWL-Your Premier Bottle cap Supplier in China,We supply a full range of liquor bottle caps.each designed for specific purposes and industries. The variety of bottle caps is influenced by factors such as the type of beverage or product being sealed, the packaging requirements, and the desired level of tamper resistance. Here are some common types of bottle caps:

Aluminum Cap: It is a sealing material specially used for alcoholic product packaging, mainly used to seal wine bottles (such as wine bottles, spirits bottles, etc.). It is not only an important part of wine packaging, but also plays a key role in protecting wine quality, enhancing brand image and enhancing consumer experience.

Aluminum-plastic cap: It is a bottle cap specially used for wine packaging. It is mainly composed of aluminum and plastic materials. The two are combined through a specific process. It uses the strength, hardness, corrosion resistance and easy processing of aluminum and the good sealing, elasticity and chemical stability of plastic to provide reliable sealing and anti-theft protection for wine products. It also has a certain decorative effect to meet the needs of wine products in storage, transportation, sales and other links, while improving the overall quality and market appeal of the product.

GPI Cap: is an anti-counterfeiting and sealing device for wine bottles, usually made of glass, embedded in the bottle mouth to ensure the authenticity and safety of the product. It performs well in anti-counterfeiting and sealing and is widely used in high-end wines to ensure product quality and brand reputation.

Screw Caps: These are widely used for sealing bottles and jars. They have threads on the inside that match the threads on the bottle neck, allowing for a secure closure.

Tinplate Cap: The primary function is sealing, which can provide a reliable sealing environment for wine products, ensuring that the wine does not evaporate or leak during storage and transportation, and maintaining the flavor and quality of the wine. At the same time, it also has certain anti-theft and anti-counterfeiting functions. Some tinplate caps are designed with anti-theft rings or special opening structures. Once the bottle cap is opened, the anti-theft device will be destroyed, indicating that the bottle cap has been opened. In addition, tinplate caps can also be printed, stamped and other processes to produce various exquisite patterns, texts and brand logos on the surface, which plays a role in promoting and beautifying products and enhancing the market appeal of wine products.

Crown Caps: Also known as bottle caps or beer caps, these are commonly used for sealing glass bottles containing carbonated beverages, particularly beer. They have a distinctive crimped edge.

Pull Ring Cap: a bottle cap used to seal wine bottles, usually made of metal, with a side pull ring design for easy opening. Side pull ring caps are widely used in ready-to-drink alcoholic beverages such as beer and pre-mixed cocktails due to their convenient opening method and good sealing performance. They are suitable for scenarios that require quick opening.

Oil cap (oil plug): a special bottle cap or bottle plug used to seal wine bottles, usually made of oily material, mainly used to enhance sealing and prevent wine leakage or oxidation. Oil caps and oil plugs are widely used in high-end wines due to their excellent sealing and lubricity, especially wines that need to be stored for a long time, to ensure the quality and flavor of the wine.

Guala Cap: usually has the characteristics of high quality and high precision, can provide good sealing performance for wine products, and ensure that the wine maintains stable quality during storage and transportation. At the same time, it also has unique advantages in appearance design and anti-counterfeiting technology, such as using advanced printing technology to make the pattern and text of the bottle cap clear and beautiful, enhancing the overall image of the product; it also uses a variety of anti-counterfeiting methods, such as special materials, structures or markings, to prevent the emergence of counterfeit and inferior products and protect the rights and interests of brands and consumers.

PVC Cap: It is a packaging accessory made of polyvinyl chloride (PVC) material, which is used to cover the mouth of the wine bottle to seal, protect and decorate the wine product.

FAQ

Q: Why is Aluminium used for bottle caps?

A: Aluminum alloy bottle cap material is a new type of bottle cap sealing material. It is not only easy to open and beautiful, but also has the function of preventing counterfeit goods. Widely used in sealing packaging of beverage bottles, wine bottles, cosmetic bottles, medicine bottles, etc.

Q: What does ROPP cap mean?

A: ROPP Cap is a type of bottle closure & its full form is Roll on Pilfer Proof. It is kind of Metal cap generally made using fine grade of Aluminium as Aluminium imparts the benefit of ductility.

Q: What is a pilfer proof cap?

A: These caps are specifically designed and possess embossed directions for application and removal. These caps are designed using the special technology of pushing the cap up and then opening the bottle as a child's general tendency is to turn the cap to open

Q: What is a screw cap called?

A: Screw caps or “Stelvin caps” are closures made only from aluminum material that threads onto the bottleneck. Compared to cork, screw caps prevent the wine faults of oxidation and of cork taint, and are easier to open.

Q: What is the difference between a flip cap and a screw cap?

A: A flip-top cap is a screw cap with added functionality. It includes an additional hinged lid that can be flipped open to reveal a spout, nozzle, or series of holes. This allows the consumer to easily access the contents of the container without having to fully remove the cap.
