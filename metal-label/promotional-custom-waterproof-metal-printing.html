Custom Metal Labels

Product Introduction:
Logo sticker labels are sticker labels used to display brand logos or specific logos. Their characteristics are: Strong identification: The core function is to highlight the logo of a brand or organization, so that consumers or audiences can quickly identify and remember the brand, which is an important part of the brand's visual image. High flexibility: Various shapes, sizes, colors and materials can be customized according to different needs to adapt to different application scenarios and product packaging. Easy to paste: With adhesive on the back, it can be easily pasted on various surfaces, such as product packaging, the product itself, promotional materials, vehicles, buildings, etc., and it is firmly pasted and not easy to fall off. Good durability: Using high-quality materials and printing technology, it has good waterproof, moisture-proof, sun-proof, anti-fading and other properties, and can keep the logo clear and complete under different environmental conditions. Strong customizability: In addition to displaying the logo, other relevant information can also be added, such as brand name, slogan, contact information, website, etc., to further strengthen brand promotion and information communication.
