Product Introduction:
Shining appearance: It has a bright golden metallic luster, which can reflect light and present a dazzling visual effect at different angles, which is eye-catching. Its embossed curved surface design increases the three-dimensional and layered sense of the label, making the pattern or text seem to protrude from the surface of the label, which is more artistic and textured. Excellent material: Aluminum is used as the base material. Aluminum has the advantages of light weight, corrosion resistance, and strong weather resistance, so that the label can maintain good stability under various environmental conditions, is not easy to deform, fade or damage, and can be used for a long time. Self-adhesive and convenient: There is a strong adhesive on the back, and no additional pasting tools are required. Just gently tear off the backing paper, and the label can be firmly pasted on various surfaces, such as glass, plastic, metal, wood, etc. The pasting process is simple and convenient, and the stickiness is long-lasting and not easy to fall off. Curved surface fitting: The special curved surface design enables it to better fit various curved objects, such as cylindrical, spherical, etc., can be tightly wrapped on the surface of the object, and perfectly fit the shape of the object, showing smooth lines and overall sense, increasing the scope of application and decorative effect of the label.
