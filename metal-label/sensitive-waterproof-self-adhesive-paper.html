Product Introduction:
Waterproof: Special paper materials or surface coatings are used to make the label resistant to water erosion. Whether in a humid environment, or when there are water stains on the bottle surface or liquid splashes on the label, the pattern, text and other information on the label can remain clear and complete. For example, when using this label on a beverage bottle or shower gel bottle, even if the bottle is soaked in water or has water droplets attached, the label will not appear blurred or faded. Self-adhesive: There is a layer of sticky material on the back of the label, which has good adhesion and initial adhesion. It can be firmly attached to the surface of the bottle, and the operation is simple during the pasting process. You only need to tear the label off the backing paper and then paste it on the surface of the bottle. At the same time, the sticky material can also adapt to bottles of different materials, such as plastic bottles, glass bottles, metal bottles, etc. Sensitivity: The sensitivity here may refer to the sensitive reaction to the contents of the bottle. For example, for some bottles containing chemical ingredients, such as medicine bottles and cosmetic bottles, the label material is compatible with the substances in the bottle, and the label will not be damaged by the leakage or chemical reaction of the substances in the bottle. Or it refers to sensitivity to environmental factors, such as labels may have a certain adaptability to environmental changes such as temperature and humidity, and will not have quality problems within a certain range. Customization: As a private label, it can be fully customized according to the brand, purpose, specifications and other information of the product in the bottle. Including the shape of the label (round, square, special shape, etc.), size, color, pattern, text, etc. For example, a small perfume bottle label can be designed into a beautiful oval shape with the brand logo, perfume name, fragrance description and instructions for use printed on it.
